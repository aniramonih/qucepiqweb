Far Cry 5: All 20 Perk Magazine Locations In Whitetail Mountains

Recommended Videos

You can obtain various perk points in Far Cry 5 by collecting collectibles. In this case, collecting perk magazine gives you one perk point. These perk points can be used to obtain ability points which will help you unlock different skills and help you progress in the game.

Perk Magazine Location In Jacob Seed’s Region (Whitetail River)

Perk Magazine 1:

Location: At the north of Jacob’s region towards the west of cedar lake. Look for a lake, at the lake near a tent you will find the magazine.

Perk Magazine 2:

Location: The second perk magazine is to the west of Cedar Lake. Across the lake, there an opening to an underground bunker, there you will find the second collectable.

Perk Magazine 3:

Location: The third magazine is located just next to Cedar Lake. It is close to the same place where you found the second magazine, but a little before, in a tent on the shelf.

Perk Magazine 4:

Location: The fourth magazine is on an island in Cedar Lake. It’s the only island in the Lake, once you get there go to the old wooden house, inside on the shelf you will find the fourth magazine.

Perk Magazine 5:

Location: On the south west region of Cedar Lake on top of the mountain, behind a sign board you will find the fifth magazine.

Perk Magazine 6:

Location: The sixth magazine is on the east of Cedar Lake, on top of a hill, inside a cave where you will find a couple dancing. The magazine is kept on a rake in the corner.

Perk Magazine 7:

Location: Towards the north of Wishbone Lake, there’s a bridge. Below the bridge you will find a sewer pipe, go inside and walk to the end you will find the magazine on the floor.

Perk Magazine 8:

Location: Go to the north of Baron Lumber Mill and east of F.A.N.G Centre on top of a hill. Once you are there, under the chair you will find the eight magazine.

Perk Magazine 9:

Location: South of Baron Lumber mill, there’s a petrol pump along the road. Go to the back of the petrol pump climb up on the roof and you will find your 9th magazine.

Perk Magazine 10:

Location: Further down south-east from Baron lumber mill at Loresca Residence, outside on the door you will find the 10th magazine.

Perk Magazine 11:

Location: South west of F.A.N.G Centre and along Moccasin River you will find a tree house, go in there and you will spot your 11th magazine.

Perk Magazine 12:

Location: West of F.A.N.G center across the moccasin river on a wooden bridge you will find your 12th magazine.

Perk Magazine 13:

Location: Your 13th Perk Magazine is at the west of Moccasin River and south west of Cedar Lake. It is at a lookout point on a tree use a grapple rope to get there.

Perk Magazine 14:

Location: On the west of Moccasin River there’s a small lake with a small island. You will find your 14th magazine on a bench there.

Perk Magazine 15:

Location: East of the magazine location 14th, just around the valley you will find your 15h magazine. It is in a bunker on a sofa.

Perk Magazine 16:

Location: West of Moccasin River and South west of Cedar Lake at the extreme left end of the map you will find a base camp. On one of the wooden piles you will find your 16th magazine.

Perk Magazine 17:

Location: Just south east of your 16th perk magazine is your 17th perk magazine, down the cliff on a tree lookout using your grapple rope to get to it.

Perk Magazine 18:

Location: North of Clagett bay after you across the road, there’s a base camp with a tree lookout, climb up the ladder and you will find your 18th perk magazine.

Perk Magazine 19:

Location: North West of widow creek and south west of Clagett bay, a little way from the road you will find a bunker. Go inside, down the stairs. Take a left from the dead body then a right through the door to the end where you will find the 19th magazine on a bed.

Perk Magazine 20:

Location: North West of Langford Lake, on the left from the road you will come across dirt road that leads to a house that has car and some furniture outside. You will find the 20th magazine near books on the floor.
