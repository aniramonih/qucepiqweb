NBC10 Reporter Tim Furlong Died: Remembering a Stellar Journalist and Friend

It is with heavy hearts that we bid farewell to Tim Furlong, an esteemed reporter for NBC10 and a beloved member of the Montreal community. After a courageous battle with cancer, Furlong passed away surrounded by his family.

A Career Dedicated to Uncovering Truths

Furlong’s journalism career spanned over two decades. During this time, he reported on significant events, from local stories in Montreal to international news events. Known for his dedication, tenacity, and empathetic approach, Furlong was a respected figure in the journalistic community.

An Inspiring Battle With Cancer

Furlong was diagnosed with cancer and fearlessly shared his journey with the public. His openness about his health struggles humanized him to viewers and made his eventual passing all the more poignant. His fight against the disease was marked by bravery and determination, inspiring many who are battling similar health issues.

A Legacy Left Behind

Furlong leaves behind a profound legacy in journalism. His dedication to uncovering the truth, holding those in power accountable, and giving a voice to the voiceless shaped his career and inspired many aspiring journalists. His loss will be deeply felt within the NBC10 family and the wider journalism community.

A Pillar of the Montreal Community

Beyond his work, Furlong was a pillar of the Montreal community. He used his platform to raise awareness about various causes and was actively involved in local charities. His commitment to making a difference extended far beyond his professional life, and his loss will undoubtedly leave a void in the community.

A Personal Loss

On a personal level, those who knew Furlong speak of his kindness, humor, and generosity. He was a devoted husband, father, and friend, known for his warmth and genuine care for others. His passing leaves a deep void, and he will be sorely missed by all who knew him.

United Irish Societies of Montreal Shared a Tribute Post on Facebook by writing:-

Today we all lost a pillar of the Montreal Irish Community and 2023 Grand Marshal of the parade – Tim Furlong. Tim passed away after a 3 year battle with cancer, which he always braved with a smile. Tim is survived by his wife Kim Lampitt, 2 sons Brad & Derrick and his mother Evelyn – please keep all of Tim’s family in your prayers. Tim, you and your contagious smile will forever be in our memories. More details to follow in the coming days.

As we remember Tim Furlong, let’s honor his legacy by striving for journalistic integrity, showing compassion towards others, and demonstrating resilience in the face of adversity. Our deepest condolences go out to his family, friends, and colleagues during this difficult time.

Rest in peace, Tim Furlong. You may be gone, but your impact and memory will live on.
