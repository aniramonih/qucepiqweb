Chris Brown Bio, Parents, Girlfriends, Kids, Net Worth Zodiac sign

One of America’s top-notch entertainers, Chris Brown who has multiple professions as an actor, songwriter, and R&B singer, is popularly known for his outstanding skills in dancing and sweet soothing voice exposing and keeping him in the limelight for more than a decade. His past chaotic lifestyle sometimes caused him to trend for the wrong reasons. 

Chris Brown Bio

On May 5, 1989, Christopher Maurice Brown who is widely known by the stage name Chris Brown was born in Tappahannock, Virginia, in the United States as the youngest child of Clinton Brown, a corrections officer at a local prison, and Joyce Hawkins, a former daycare center director. He grew up in his hometown alongside his one and only older sister  Lytrell Bundy. At a very young age, Chris gained a passion for music and dance and often listened to soul albums belonging to his parents. He refers to the King of Pop, the late Michael Jackson as his source of inspiration. 

He had his high school education at Essex High School in Virginia. 

In 2004, Chris got signed with the record label, Jive Records and released his self-titled debut studio album, Chris Brown album in 2015 which was a commercial success, topping number two on the Billboard 200 within the first week selling over 154,000 copies, and over 300 million copies in the States only and six million copies worldwide. He released his second album Exclusive in 2007 which was also successful. 

However, his chaotic lifestyle had a negative effect on his music career. He assaulted elite R&B singer Rihanna in the year 2009, at a time when she was his lover. Due to that, he was arrested. It had an effect on his third album Graffiti which was a commercial failure. 

He rose to the ladder once again, when he released his fourth album F.A.M.E. in 2011 which topped the chart on the Billboard 200. There were several hit songs in the album some of which are Beautiful People, Look at Me Now, and Yeah 3x which made him a recipient of a Grammy Award. 

He’s not only known for music but has done well with his career in the movie industry as an actor. He’s starred in a number of films like Stomp the Yard, Battle of the Year, Think Like a Man, Takers, and This Christmas. 

For his good works in the entertainment industry, he’s won numerous accolades for them like a Grammy Award, eighteen BET Awards, and four Billboard Music Awards.
