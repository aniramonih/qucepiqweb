Donny Osmond Is a Loving Father of 5 Children

Donny Osmond’s children are five in number, and they include Donald Clark Jr., Jeremy James, Brandon Michael, Christopher Glenn, and Joshua Davis Osmond. Donny Osmond had all his five sons with the only love of his life, his wife, Debra Osmond.

The Osmond boys are all grown up and forging their own part in life, and many of them opted to follow professions outside the entertainment industry. While Donald works as a public relations officer, Jeremy is an expert in physical therapy, and Brandon is an art director, though he dabbles into acting and music occasionally. The only son of the entertainer that seems to be following in his footsteps is Christopher, who is already creating a niche for himself in the music world. Joshua, the last child of Donny and Debra, is into missionary work.

Meet Donny Osmond’s Children

Donald Clark Osmond Jr.

  • Date of birth: July 31, 1979
  • Age: 44 years old
  • Mother: Debra Osmond
  • Occupation: Public relations officer

Donny Osmond has five biological children, and the first one is Donald Osmond Clark Jr., Born on the 31st of July 1979. The American actor and his lifelong wife, Debra Osmond, became first-time parents when they welcomed Donald in Salt Lake Temple, Utah, in the United State. The celebrity kid’s place of birth is an indication of his American nationality, but his ethnic background is Caucasian.

Donald has not revealed anything about his academic journey, but he is said to have attended elementary and high school in his locality. He also went all the way to college, but the name of his academic institution and course of study has never been revealed.

Donald Clark Osmond Jr is a PR Guru

Donald Clark Osmond Jr. is quite unlike his entertainment icon father as he chooses to follow a completely different career path. According to Donny Osmond’s first son, he was sick and tired of the kind of fame and popularity that followed his dad and uncle, as it brought him a lot of bad experiences. “It ate me up”, Donald said. Thus, when the time came for him to choose a career path, he went to pitch his tent in the public relations field, becoming “the spin doctor of all spin.”

The Spectrum revealed that Donny Osmond’s oldest son owns and runs his own public relations firm in the US. He is also connected to OzComm Marketing as a managing partner thanks to his experience in business marketing. Donald has his father on his client list.

The media PR officer may have rejected everything about fame, but that has not stopped him from making brief onscreen appearances from time to time. In 2012, he appeared in a preview clip on The Talk alongside his movie icon dad. According to the father-son duo, their intention was to spin a surprise on the very special ladies of the program for Father’s Day.

He is Happily Married to Jessica Nelson

Donald Clark Osmond Jr. has been married for over one decade. His better half has been identified as Jessica Nelson, and they exchanged wedding vows in Oquirrh Mountain Utah Temple, South Jordan, in 2010.

From what is perceivable, Donald’s marriage is beginning to look like the “happily ever after kind of union, just like what his parents enjoy. What’s more, he and Jessica are already parents to four lovely kids. They welcomed their last child in 2020, making it the 12th grandchild for Donny Osmond, who couldn’t contain his joy. The entertainment icon took to social media to announce that they were officially a dozen amidst congratulations from fans.

Jeremy James Osmond

  • Date of birth: June 8, 1981
  • Age: 42 years old
  • Mother: Debra Osmond
  • Occupation: Physiotherapist

The name of Donny Osmond’s second child is Jeremy James Osmond, and he was born on the 8th of June 1981, which pegs his current age at 42. Jeremy is just a couple of years younger than his big brother, Donald Jr. An American national, he was also born in Utah, and his ethnicity is equally Caucasian. Donny had Jeremy from his marriage with Debra.

Though he is the type that loves to live their life away from public scrutiny, it has come to the fore that Jeremy is well educated. He started his academic journey in Utah and went on to acquire a Ph.D. in physical therapy.

Jeremy Osmond Works as a Physical Therapist

Jeremy is another of Donny Osmond’s children that declined to follow him into the entertainment industry. He preferred to pursue a career in the health sector, precisely in the physiotherapy field. He is currently employed as a physiotherapy expert in Lindon, Utah, where he is raising his family.

Jeremy is Married with Children

Jeremy James Osmond happens to be the first Osmond son to get married. He wedded his wife, Melisa Osmond, in 2002, eight years before his older brother, Donald.

He and Melisa have been married for over two decades, and they contributed three of the entrainment icon’s 12 grandchildren. Jeremy and Melisa are parents to two boys and one girl – Dylan James in 2005, Ryder James, who came in 2013, and daughter Emery (b. 2008).

Donny Osmond once took to his Instagram to gush about his cute granddaughter, describing her as his sweet princess; Donny said Emery is radiant on the inside and outside and growing up very fast. He also commended her for growing into a strong woman which gives him an immense sense of pride “Bapa loves you, Emy”, he ended the post.

Brandon Michael Osmond

  • Date of birth: 29th January 1985
  • Age: 38 years old
  • Mother: Debra Osmond
  • Occupation: Musician, actor, photographer, art designer, and advertiser

Born on the 29th of January 1985, Brandon Michael Osmond is the third child of Donny Osmond and his wife, Debra Osmond. Just like his siblings before him, Brandon’s place of birth is captured as Utah, making him a citizen of the United States, and his ethnicity is Caucasian. Brandon is four years younger than his immediate older brother, Jeremy, and six years younger than Donald Jr.

Brandon attended high school in his Utah locality, and upon graduation, the third child of Donny Osmond proceeded to Utah Valley University to study Photography and Graphics, graduating in 2009. His quest for further academic accolades took him to Bringham Yong University to acquire a Bachelor’s degree in communication

Donny Osmond’s third Son is a Musician, Actor, and Photographer

Brandon Michael Osmond is multi-talented; he is listed as a musician, actor, art designer, advertiser, and photographer.

After he completed his studies, Brandon joined BYU AdLab in 2010 as an Art Director – a position he held for a couple of years till 2012. At BYU AdLab, he was afforded the opportunity to put his knowledge in art directing to the test for the first time.

Before his stint at BYU AdLab, Brandon tried his hands at becoming an entrepreneur by launching the Brandon Osmond Design, which has remained a thriving venture to date. Donny’s third son once worked with Salt Lake City in his capacity as the firm’s Senior Art Director. Currently, he holds a lucrative position at Radiant as the Creative Director. Brandon started working in the company in 2020 and is still with them.

A veritable jack of all trades, Brandon dabbles in acting, music, photography, and everything that is connected to creativity. If what his wife, Shelby Hansen, said is anything to go by, then Brandon has the natural capacity to imitate any accent or voice of any character.

Brandon is also a blogger. He collaborates with his significant other, Shelby, to run a blog site where they post a lot of things, including their life experiences, their children, childbirth, and whatever you can think of.

Brandon Michael Osmond is a Father of Three

In 2008, Donny Osmond’s third son became the husband of Shelby Hansen Osmond. Brandon even got married two years before his oldest brother Donald and his marriage has been going strong for over 14 years and still counting.

The couple is parents to four children – all boys. They are Daxton Osmond, Tayte Osmond, Benson Osmond, and Peder Osmond.

Christopher Glenn Osmond

  • Date of birth: 12th December 1990
  • Age: 33 years old
  • Mother: Debra Osmond
  • Occupation: Photographer, music artist, sound mixer

Donny Osmond’s fourth son, Christopher Glenn Osmond, was born on the 12th of December 1990. Chris holds a very special place in his dad’s heart, as the duo’s birthdays are just days apart. Thus, they often celebrate together. The fact that he is almost a birthday mate with his son seems to be a big hit with the entertainment heavyweight. He took to Instagram in December 2020 to gush about how much he loves celebrating with Chris, “You are the best birthday gift I’ve ever received,” Donny concluded.

Like his siblings, Chris was also born in Utah and is an American citizen. He equally has Caucasian ethnicity.

The name of the academic institutions Chris attended is not known, but reports said he attended a private school in Utah for his high school education and also passed through a reputable college in the United States. Though this is yet to be confirmed, there are reports that he majored in music at the university level.

Donny and Debra often pop over to visit Chris and his wife, and the actor captured one of such moments via Instagram.

Donny Osmond’s Fourth Son Appears to be following his Footsteps

Christopher Glenn Osmond is listed as a musician, photographer, and Sound Mixer.

He has a job with Disney, where he functions as a sound mixer. Christopher is seriously pursuing a career in the music industry and is often spotted singing alongside his superstar father. Whenever he sings with Donny, fans always say they see a younger version of the music icon in Chris.

He formed a music band with his peers, where he functions as the band’s guitarist. When country music star Collin Raye performed at a free concert hosted in Salt Lake City, Chris was his opening act.

He has already dropped his first single, Calling in 2019, followed by two other tracks, Up To You and Dreamer.

Christopher Glenn Osmond is a Family Man

Christopher’s relationship status changed in May 2017 when he exchanged wedding vows with Alta Osmond, and on November 27, 2020, the couple became proud parents of a baby girl, Aussie Rae Osmond.

Aussie is Donny Osmond’s second granddaughter and his 11th grandchild. The entertainment heavyweight, who has always been used to having only boys in the family, was over the moon with the birth of his second granddaughter. The dotting grandfather shared a picture of the proud parents with their baby girl with the caption, “Thanksgiving is extra special this year. Aussie is here and she is so beautiful.”

Chris recently took his baby girl to Disneyland where father and daughter were spotted in a happy mood.

Joshua Davis Osmond 

  • Date of birth: 16th February 1998
  • Age: 25 years old
  • Mother: Debra Osmond
  • Occupation: Missionary

Donny Osmond’s youngest son, Joshua Davis Osmond, was born on the 16th of February 1998, which pegs his current age at 25. An American national, Josh was also born in Utah, and he is Caucasian by ethnicity.

Like his siblings, Josh passed through Schools in Utah, both high school and university, but the names of the academic institutions are not known.

Josh is a Missionary

Joshua Davis Osmond has shown zero interest in following his father into the entertainment industry; rather, the young man prefers to work as a missionary.

In 2016, he made the move from the United States to Rome to work with The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints as a missionary. He stayed in Italy for two long years without seeing any member of his family, and when he reunited with his father in December 2018, the movie icon marveled at his accomplishments.

The dotting papa shared an Instagram picture of himself, Josh, and Debbie, which he captioned. “A wonderful reunion in Rome!” the movie icon and music star raved about his son being away from the family for two years to work for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints as well as the fact that he was coming back to spend the holidays with them.

Donny Osmond’s Last Son got Married in 2020

Despite the chaos created by the Corona Virus pandemic, Joshua Davis Osmond managed to tie the knot with his fiancee, Summer Felsted, in June 2020. Because of the ravaging effects of the pandemic in America, the duo was joined in a private ceremony in his dad’s backyard. The moment was described as simple yet very memorable for the newlyweds.

Donny, who never misses an opportunity to gush over his children and their achievements, wasted no time in sharing their wedding picture via his IG handle and wrote, “Today was a picture-perfect day. Summer and Josh, we are absolutely thrilled for you and your bright future ahead.”

Talking about the venue of their wedding, Josh told Closer that it made their day more personal and special, and the ceremony, he said, turned out to be more beautiful beyond their imaginations.

The couple was barely two years into their marriage when they became first-time parents; Josh and Summer welcomed the first child of their union, Beckham, in September 2022.
