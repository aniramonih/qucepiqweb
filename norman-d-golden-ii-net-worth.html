What is Norman D. Golden II's Net Worth?

Norman D. Golden II is an American actor and rapper who has a net worth of $200 thousand. Norman D. Golden II was born in April 1984. He first starred as Twist in the television series True Colors in 1990.

He starred as Devon Butler in the movie Cop and ½ in 1993. He also appeared in the 1997 movie Gone Fishin'. Golden also appeared in the TV series Sisters and the TV mini-series Moby Dick as well as the TV movies There Are No Children Here, On Promised Land, America's Dream, and Shaughnessy.
