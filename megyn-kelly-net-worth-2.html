Controversy

Throughout her career, Megyn Kelly has been no stranger to controversy. Her outspoken nature and willingness to tackle controversial topics have garnered both praise and criticism.

One of the most notable controversies surrounding Megyn Kelly occurred during her time at NBC. In 2018, she faced intense backlash for making controversial statements about race while discussing the use of blackface as part of a Halloween costume on her show, “Megyn Kelly Today.” Her comments were widely criticized for being insensitive and racially insensitive, sparking a heated debate.

“But what is racist? Because you get in trouble if you are a white person who puts on blackface on Halloween, or a black person who puts on white face for Halloween…Back when I was a kid, that was okay, as long as you were dressing up as a character.”

The controversy surrounding her remarks led to swift condemnation and outrage from viewers, fellow journalists, and activists alike. The incident significantly impacted Megyn Kelly’s reputation and credibility as a journalist.

The aftermath of this controversy ultimately led to the cancellation of “Megyn Kelly Today” and the termination of her contract with NBC. Despite issuing apologies and attempting to clarify her intentions, the damage had been done.

It is important to note that this was not the first time Megyn Kelly found herself embroiled in controversy. Throughout her career, she has faced criticism, particularly for her statements on sensitive topics.
