Moon Bin Age & Early Life

Moon Bin was born in Cheongju, Chungbuk Province, on January 26, 1998. He was 25 years old (at the time of death). Moon has two siblings, the other being his younger sister, Moon Sua, a member of the South Korean girl group Billlie. He studied at and graduated from Hanlim Multi Art School, where he specialized in Practical Music.

Moon Bin’s Net Worth

How much is Moon Bin’s net worth? Moonbin’s net worth at the time of his death is unclear, however being a prominent K-Pop musician and actor, he was most certainly well-off. Career

Moonbin made his debut as a child model and ulzzang in 2004 thanks to his mother’s assistance. His first known appearance was in TVXQ’s music video for “Balloons” in 2006 when he played a little U-Know Yunho. He made his acting debut in the Korean drama Boys over Flowers in 2009, playing the younger version of Kim Bum’s character.

Moonbin has been a Fantagio trainee since the fifth grade, eventually becoming a full-fledged trainee in his middle school years. He took part in the company’s boy band initiative iTeen and was the second trainee to be given the Fantagio iTeen Photo Test Cut. Moonbin and the rest of the group featured in a web drama named To Be Continued before making their Astro debut.

Moonbin is a member of Astro, a six-member boy band that released their first extended play, Spring Up, with five songs, including the lead, hit “Hide & Seek.” He appeared on The Ultimate Watchlist of Latest Trends for the first two seasons and was recently cast in Moments of Eighteen. Moonbin confirmed their absence from the forthcoming promotions for their Blue Flame EP release on November 12, 2019.

Moonbin was chosen as one of the fashion models for the brand “Nerdy Cafe” after joining the eco-friendly culinary show Food Avengers. On September 3, 2021, Neikidnis announced that Moonbin had been chosen as its first muse, and he was cast in the second season of Coupang Play’s Saturday Night Live Korea. Fantagio stated on December 30, 2022, that Moonbin has elected to continue his contract with the agency.

Death

He was discovered dead at his residence in Seoul’s Gangnam area on the evening of April 19, 2023. His manager tracked him down when he failed to show up for rehearsals. He was 25. According to a statement made by the Seoul Gangnam Police, who are investigating the deceased, “it appears that he committed suicide.” We are presently exploring the option of doing an autopsy to ascertain the exact cause of death.” On April 20, Gangnam Police informed CNN that “no signs of foul play have been found related to this case.”

He was scheduled to play at the Dream Concert in Busan with fellow Astro member Sanha as part of their duet, Moonbin & Sanha, only a day before his death. As of April 20, the organizers had not issued a statement in response to his death.
