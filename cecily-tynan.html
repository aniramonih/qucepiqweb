The meteorologist Cecily Tynan Biography, Husband, Net Worth, Children

Cecily Tynan is an American TV meteorologist who is working for WPVI-TV, ABC channel 6 since 1995. There she is 5, 6, and 11, pm chief weathercaster. 

The weather woman Cecily Joan was born on March 19, 1969, in Newton, Connecticut, USA. Tynan is very close to her beloved mother with whom she shares several pictures on her social accounts. She has a brother named Bob Tynan. Concerning education, Cecily earned a degree in journalism & politics from Washington and Lee University.

Relationship Status: Married

The 51-years-old Cecily is in a marital relationship with Greg Watson. Nothing much is known about Greg's professional work. But he is a runner who takes part in several marathon events. 

How and when Cecily met her now-husband Greg it is unknown. But it seems like the duo dated several months before they merrily tied the wedding knot in April 2005. 

As of 2020, the husband-wife duo is celebrating their 15-years of marriage anniversary happily without creating any disputes related to the separation. Additionally, they spend their vacation together and are now parenting their kids jointly.

From the marital bond, the married pair shared two adorable children. They welcomed their first kid, a son, Luke Watson in January 2006. Similarly, they became parents for the second time in September 2007, who shared a daughter named Emma Watson.

As of now, the Watson family of four is living a lavish and limelight life. Not only that but the nuclear happiest family also have three pet dogs Nala, Sandy, and Kiwi.

Former Affair

Before sharing an intimate life with Greg, the weathercaster Cecily was previously married to the school teacher Michael Badger. 

Where and when Michael & Cecily got married it is still under the radar. But as per some sources, the former husband-wife duo divorced in 2004. On the other hand, there is no information available regarding Greg's former romantic relationships.
