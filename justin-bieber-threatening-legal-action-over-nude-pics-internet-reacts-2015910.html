Justin Bieber Threatening Legal Action Over Nude Pics: Internet Reacts

Justin Bieber and his team are not happy about the nude photos published this week from his recent vacation in Bora Bora. On Thursday, Oct. 8, Bieber's legal representatives sent a cease and desist letter to the New York Daily News, which printed and posted the nude photos on Wednesday.

According to The Hollywood Reporter, which obtained a copy of the letter, 21-year-old Bieber's team demanded that all photos of the "What Do You Mean?" chart topper and model Jayde Pierce — including a gallery of 95 images — be removed within 12 hours. As of Friday morning, the photos were still on NYDN's site.

"We recently became informed that your company has obtained and is distributing unauthorized photographs of our Client including images showing him without clothing," the letter stated (according to THR).
