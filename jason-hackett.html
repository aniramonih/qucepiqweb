Jason Hackett Wiki (KOCO-TV Anchor) Age, Wife, Biography &amp; More

Jason Hackett (born in 1982) is an American Journalist, News Anchor, Television Personality, and Reporter from Florida. He works as a KOCO 5 News Morning anchor. He started his career many years and used to work with KSGF-FM network in Springfield, Missouri.

Later, Jason began serving as a journalist for Garden Center Magazine. He also worked for one of the leading trade publications named “Greenhouse Management”. He also worked for various radio stations like KFAQ-AM and WTMJ-AM.

Some of his other appearances in the numerous platforms are KAMR-TV (Amarillo, TX), KIVI-TV, KERO-TV, KGUN-TV, WFTX-TV, KSHB-TV, WFTS-TV, WCPO-TV, etc. Recently, Hackett involved in a controversy after his co-anchor made a racist “Gorilla” remark.

Full Story – His Co-host Alex Housden Compared him to a Gorilla

According to Wikipedia, the incident took place on August 22, 2019, when he and Alex Housden were reacting to a light-hearted video from the Oklahoma City Zoo. It was a video about an ape named Fin who was getting close to the camera.

Jason and Housden were laughing over Fin during the morning program on KOCO-TV on Thursday. Suddenly, Alex ended the segment by commenting that the gorilla kinda looks like him.

A facebook user “Joy Shana” recorded the remark and uploaded it on the internet. The video went viral on the internet immediately. She received many hate messages on Facebook.

Moreover, one person said that whether Alex finds all Afro-American look like gorillas. The next day, the co-anchors appeared on the same show in order to address the issue. Alex emotionally accepted that she said something inconsiderate and inappropriate.

Then, she turned to Hackett and told him that she loves him a lot and would never hurt his feeling purposely. She also said that she apologized from the bottom of her heart to all the people who got offended by her improper words.
