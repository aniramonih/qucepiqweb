Edward Norton, Shauna Robertson's Baby Name Is Atlas, Son Is 22 Months

He's finally on the map! Almost two years after Edward Norton and Shauna Robertson welcomed their first child together, the little one's name has finally been revealed. 

Sources confirm to Us Weekly that the couple's son is named Atlas. As Us exclusively revealed in April 2013, movie producer Robertson gave birth to their baby boy in March 2013. 

 

Oscar-nominated Birdman actor Norton, 45, has been famously tight-lipped about his wife and son. He chose not to confirm the baby's birth at the time, and releases little information about his personal life. 

The Fight Club star wed his filmmaker love in secret in 2012, as Us exclusively revealed in 2013. The private couple exchanged vows months before the birth of Atlas, giving little public fanfare to the nuptials.
