Imagine Dragons Biography: Songs, Members And Genre

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada in the United States of America. The band consists of Dan Reynolds (Lead singer), Wayne Sermon (Lead Guitarist), Ben McKee (Bassist), and Daniel Platzman (Drummer).

Imagine Dragons began to function in 2008 but gained popularity in 2012 with their single “It’s Time”. The Song was well celebrated by the Americans and it spread out to other countries.

Imagine Dragons band members came together as students in the university and has since stayed together to create good music. There were many band members of Imagine Dragons but some of them departed along their journey to fame.

In 2012, Imagine Dragons gained recognition with their single “It’s Time” followed by their award-winning album “Night Visions”, which was released in 2012. The Album consists of top hits like “Radioactive,” and “Demons”. Radioactive was one of the songs of the Album that kept “It’s Time” album for weeks on the Billboard Hot 100 and it also won the biggest rock hit of the year.
