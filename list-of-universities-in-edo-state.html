List of Universities in Edo State

Edo State federal, state and private universities.

Seven universities exist in Edo State, of which one belongs to the federal government, two belong to the state government while four are privately-owned.

Below is the list of NUC approved Federal, state and private universities in Edo State.

Universities in Edo State

Federal:

  • University of Benin

State:

  • Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma
  • Edo University Iyamo

Private:

  • Benson Idahosa University, Benin City
  • Igbinedion University Okada
  • Samuel Adegboyega University, Ogwa.
  • Wellspring University, Evbuobanosa – Edo State
