As of her career, she started her career with the NBC morning show, “The Today Show”. In 1963, she made her debut in the movies with ‘New Friendship’. From 1965 to 1967, she performed on The Ed Sullivan Show four times with her husband, Xavier Cugat.

As she was Spanish and her English was not proper which was played as a comic focus and she began using “cuchi-cuchi” in the public arena. She appeared eight times on The Love Boat in the 1970s. After that, she made her appearance in some talk shows such as The Captain and Tennille, The John Davidson Show, Donny & Marie, Tony Orlando, and Dawn, The Mike Douglas Show, and the short-lived Brady Bunch variety spinoff.

In the 2000s, she appeared in commercials like Sprint wireless phone service and GEICO insurance. On 11th May 2008, she appeared as a guest on the VH1 reality show Viva Hollywood. In January 2008, she appeared as the Celebrity Showtime entertainer aboard Royal Caribbean Explorer of the Seas.

At the 2009 Muscular Dystrophy Association Jerry Lewis Labor Day, she performed Rihanna‘s song “Don’t Stop the Music”. In 2010, she made her appearance in Dancing with the Stars as a guest. From 1976 to 2013, she released 12 singles including “Sha Na Na”, “Hot Love”, “La Salsa”, “La Mojada”, and ”Sexy Sexy”.

From 1963 to 2017, she appeared in several movies such as “The Big Mouth”, “Thumbelina”, and “Sharknado 5: Global Swarming”. She appeared as a guest in the TV series “Jane The Virgin” and “RuPaul’s Drag Race” in 2016.

In 2017, she was one of the contestants on season 24 of Dancing with the Stars where she paired with professional dancer Keo Motsepe. She finished at 11th place and became the second couple to get eliminated from the competition.

Reflecting on her personality, she got married twice in her life. In August 1966, she got married for the first time to Xavier Cugat. When they got married, she was under 20 and her husband was 66 years old.

After became a naturalized citizen of the United States in 1977, she filed for divorce from Cugat and stated that her marriage with Cugat was a business contract. In 1978, she tied the marriage knot with Kjell Rasten with whom she blessed with a son 1981 named Shel Rasten.
