Who is Truice Young?

Truice Young is a famous American celebrity son and Music Producer.

Truice Young was born in 1997, in Los Angeles, California, the United States is now a mere 24 years old adult.

Likewise, his parents are a rapper, Dr. De (Andre Romelle Young), and his mother, Nicole Young. Later, his parents got a divorce.
