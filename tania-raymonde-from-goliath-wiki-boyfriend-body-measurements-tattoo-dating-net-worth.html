Who is Tania Raymonde?

Born Tania Raymonde Helen Katz, she is an American actress who started her professional acting career by starring in “Syd in Wonderland”, and in the “Malcolm in the Middle” TV series as Cynthia Sanders, appearing in the series run from 2000 and 2002. Her next role was that of Alex Rousseau in the series “Lost”, between 2006 and 2010, and the following year she starred in the “Death Valley” film as Carla Rinaldi. Tania has appeared on numerous films making her a very popular actress. Tania is also known for her appearance in the “Texas Chainsaw 3D”.

Professional Career

Apart from the aforementioned, Raymonde has appeared in several other movies and television series, including “Providence”, “The Nightmare Room”, “The Brother Garcia”, “That’s So Raven”, “Lost”, “The Guardian”, “NCIS”, and “Medium”. Her first feature movie role was in 2002, when she appeared in the “Children on Their Birthdays” film, then the following year starred in the “The O’Keefes” sitcom, playing Lauren O’Keefe. Tania also gained popularity when she starred in the film “Lost” as Alex Rousseau who was Benjamin Linus’s adopted daughter.

In 2006 she starred in “Garage”, and in 2008 was in three films, namely “Japan”, “The Other Side of the Tracks”, and “Chasing 3000”. The following year she featured in the film “Elsewhere”, and reprised her role in the Starz series “Crash” beside the late Dennis Hopper. She also got a chance for a recurring role in the sixth season of “Case Gold”, as Frankie Rafferty.
