Catelynn Lowell Shares Goofy Photo of &quot;Twins&quot; Tyler and Novalee

She takes after her daddy! Teen Mom OG stars Catelynn Lowell and Tyler Baltierra have been cherishing every moment with their baby girl Novalee Reign, whom they welcomed on New Year’s Day. 

And now that the little one is nearly 4 months old, she’s starting to show some real personality. On Monday, April 20, Lowell, 23, shared a split shot with her fiancé and daughter making similar goofy faces via Instagram.

“She is his twin @tylerbaltierramtv,” the proud mom captioned the cute picture. 

While the pair have been posting endless pictures and videos of their little girl on social media, their story line on Teen Mom OG revolves around their restrictions in sharing images of their first daughter Carly.

In the premiere season of Teen Mom OG, Carly’s adoptive parents Brandon and Teresa made it clear that they don’t want photos of their daughter on Twitter, Instagram, or any other platforms — a point that’s been hard for Baltierra to accept. In the April 20 episode, he noted that they have a fear that Brandon and Teresa won’t let the reality stars visit Carly any more if they cross a line.
