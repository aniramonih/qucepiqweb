Early Life

Zain was born on the 12th of January in 1993 in the city of Bradford, West Yorkshire, to Yaser and Tricia Brannan Malik. His father is a British Pakistani, and his mother is of Irish descent. Zayn has three sisters, namely Doniya, Waliyha, and Safaa.

He went to Lower Fields Primary School and Tong High School. He had initially contemplated a career as an English teacher before appearing on X-Factor.

Personal Life

In 2012, Zayn Malik dated Perrie Edwards of Little Mix. They were engaged as well, but they broke it off by 2013.

He has been dating model Gigi Hadid since 2015. She appeared on his video for “Pillowtalk,” and they are the third couple that Vogue has featured on its cover page.

Zain is also the official ambassador for British Asian Trust, which aims to help disadvantaged people residing in South Asia.

Age, Height, and Weight

Being born on 12 January 1993, Zayn Malik is 31 years old as of today’s date 21st July 2024. His height is 1.75 m tall, and his weight is 66 kg.

Career

In 2010, Zain had auditioned for X Factor. He did qualify for the competition but was soon eliminated. However, he was brought back into a group consisting of fellow contestants, Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Liam Payne, and Louis Tomlinson. Together, they finished third. They were signed by Simon Cowell into Syco Records. They eventually formed the hit boyband One Direction. In America, they signed a deal with Columbus Records.

One Direction’s debut album, Up All Night, was released in 2011. Songs like “What Makes You Beautiful,” “Gotta Be You” and “One Thing” became commercial hits both in the US and UK.

In 2012, they released their second studio album titled Take Me Home. The song, “Live while we’re young” from this album became their highest-peaking song in numerous countries.

Their third studio album, Midnight Memories, was also a commercial success.

He left One Direction in 2015 and signed a new deal with RCA Records.

His first single, “Pillowtalk,” from his solo debut album, Mind of Mine, charted number one in both the US and the UK. The album was eventually released in March 2016 and debuted in the number one position in multiple countries.

In 2017, he released the singles, “Still Got Time” and “Dusk till Dawn” which were well received by the masses.

Malik also has his own shoe line with Giuseppe Zanotti.

He was also featured in the Versace Spring Campaign in 2017.

Malik was also hired as the creative head of a joint venture between himself and Versace, titled Zayn x Versus.

Awards and Achievements

In 2015, he won the Asian Award for outstanding contribution to music.

He is also the recipient of two Teen Choice Awards in 2016.

He received a BMI London award consecutively in 2016 and 2017.

Malik also won the iHeart Music Award in 2016.

In 2017, he won a Billboard Music Award, an MTV Video Music Award and an MTV Europe Music Award.
