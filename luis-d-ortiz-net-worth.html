Luis D. Ortiz Biography

Luis D. Ortiz was born on November 13, 1986, in Guaynabo, Puerto Rico. He grew up with his mother, Mariam, his father, Daniel, and his twin brother, also named Daniel. When Luis was 16 years old, he and his brother bought one-way tickets to Florida without telling their parents. Luis found a job at a Hollister clothing store in the stockroom and also worked as a janitor at a college. In 2006, Luis moved to New York to attend the New York Film Academy. At the age of 19, he made a short film called, Amalia in Bolivia. He wrote, directed, produced, and edited the film, which won the Best Direction award at the 2007 Puerto Rico Film Festival. In 2008, Luis raised money from restaurant owners to shoot another film called, The Theatre of the Absurd in New York. However, he wasn’t satisfied with the final result and decided not to release it.
