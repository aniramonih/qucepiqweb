Early Life

David Caruso, whose full name is David Stephen Caruso, was born on 7th January 1956 in Forest Hills, USA. His parents are Joan and Charles Caruso. His father worked as an editor, and his mother worked as a librarian. His parents separated when he was quite young.

David Caruso did his schooling from Lady Queen School and later went to Molloy High School. After graduating from the Molloy high school, David worked as an usher in movie theatres.

David started his acting career by doing small roles in movies. He got his major break in the TV series NYPD and has since then acted in various movies and TV shows.
