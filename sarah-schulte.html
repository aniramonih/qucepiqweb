Sarah Schulte Biography | Wiki

Sarah Schulte is an American journalist currently working as a general assignment reporter for ABC 7 News since 1998. Previously, Sarah served as a reporter at WCAU-TV in Philadelphia, Pa. from 1995 to 1998.

Prior to that, Sarah served as a reporter at WSMV-TV in Nashville, Tenn. (1992 -95) and at Westinghouse Broadcasting in Washington, D.C. (1990-92). Recently in her career, Sarah reported for KLAS-TV in Las Vegas, Nevada (1989-90). From 1986 to 1988, she served as a field producer and reporter for Capitol Broadcast News in Washington, D.C.
