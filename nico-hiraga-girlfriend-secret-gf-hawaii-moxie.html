Nico Hiraga Girlfriend - Secret GF In Hawaii Of The Moxie Actor!

Born and raised in San Francisco, California, Nico Hiraga was a different kid growing up which resulted in his taking up skateboarding. Since then Nico has fostered hope of going pro while also enjoying a massive following on social media and taking steps in the world of acting. Now, he is starring in Netflix movie Moxie and dating a secret girlfriend.

Nico Hiraga is a 23-year-old skateboarder, YouTuber, and now a successful actor with multiple teen movies under his belt. He is of mixed race and he always felt like an outsider which resulted in him giving up soccer and taking up skating. Saggy pants, fun-loving nature, and no shirt were an attire no parents could tolerate and now it has become a brand for the young actor.

Skating has brought Nico a lot of fame. He is endorsed by big brands and even started designing for Lakai Shoes. The online fame soon translated into him getting a Hollywood manager and now Nico is a full-fledged actor. But despite his openness about almost everything, the actor has been especially protective about his love-life, with his girlfriend.
