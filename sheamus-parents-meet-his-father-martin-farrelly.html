Sheamus Life And Career

Stephen Farrelly, whose ring name is Sheamus, is an Irish wrestler cum actor, who wresles under the WWE.

Born on January 28, 1978, Sheamus grew up in Dublin and attended Scoil Caoimhin Primary and the Gaelscoil Coláiste Mhuire Secondary School, where he was deeply involved in sports.

Sheamus later obtained a Diploma from the National College of Ireland, and went ahead to work as a night club security guard and a body guard to a number of notable celebrities.

Sheamus began to compete in wrestling on the European independent circuit. He was listed in the WWE’s main roster in 2009, and afterwards, won the world championship four times.

Sheamus is a three-time WWE Champion, a WWE’s World Heavyweight Champion, and the first Irish world champion in the history of the WWE.

A five-time tag team champion, Sheamus has a record of winning the King of the Ring tournament, the Royal Rumble match, and the Money in the Bank ladder match.
