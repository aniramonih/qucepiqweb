Granblue Fantasy Versus: Rising

Granblue Fantasy Versus: Rising is a fighting game made by Arc System Works and sold by Cygames. It's like a part two to Granblue Fantasy Versus. This game came out all over the world on December 14, 2023, and you can play it on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, and Windows.

Article continues below advertisementArticle continues below advertisement

In this game, you fight in a 2.5D world, which means it looks 3D but you only move side to side, like in classic fighting games. The goal is to beat your opponent using special moves and combos. It's not just about fighting; there's a story and different modes to play.

People who liked the first Granblue Fantasy Versus might enjoy this one too. It's available on different platforms, so many players can try it out. The game was made to continue the story and fun from the first one, giving players more of what they liked before.

Granblue Fantasy Versus: Rising Gameplay

In the gameplay of Granblue Fantasy Versus: Rising, there are some new things to make the game more exciting. They added new moves like Dash Attacks, and they changed the Auto Combo to be even better, now calling it Triple Attack. They made the netcode better, which is like the way the game connects online.

It now has rollback, which helps the online play feel smoother, and you can play with people on different platforms, thanks to crossplay. Granblue Fantasy Versus: Rising also has fun little games you can play while waiting or just for fun. There's one called "Rising Royale" and another called "Gold Brick Hoarder" that you can enjoy in the game's lobby.

Granblue Fantasy Versus Rising Tier List 2023 - FAQs

1. What is the Granblue Fantasy Versus Rising S-Tier List?

The S-Tier List for Granblue Fantasy Versus highlights exceptionally powerful characters such as Vaseraga, Avatar Belial, Vira, Yuel, Percival, Narmaya, and Grimnir, who can significantly influence the outcome of battles.

2. Why are these characters considered S-Tier?

S-Tier characters are regarded as powerful and capable of turning the tide of battle in your favor due to their exceptional skills, versatility, and overall dominance in various situations.

3. Who is included in the Rising A-Tier List, and why are they notable?

Characters like Ferry, Lowain, Cagliostro, Belial, Bubs, Eustace, Nier, Beelzebub, Metera, Charlotta, Zooey, and Ladiva are part of the A-Tier. They exhibit strong performance and versatility, making them reliable choices across different match-ups.

4. Are A-Tier characters suitable for beginners?

Yes, A-Tier characters are recommended for beginners, as they possess the ability to hold their ground against opponents and are reliable choices for a variety of match-ups.

5. Who makes up the Rising B-Tier List, and why are they recommended for beginners?  

Anila, Lancelot, Seox, Djeeta, Katalina, Gran, Soriz, and Cagliostro are part of the B-Tier. These characters are considered good for beginners because they can effectively stand their ground against enemies.
