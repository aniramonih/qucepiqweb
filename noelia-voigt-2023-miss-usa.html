7 interesting facts about the 2023 Miss USA Noelia Voigt

Source: Instagram

  • Noelia Voigt will be representing the United States at the Miss Universe 2023 Pageant.
  • Miss Utah Noelia Voigt was crowned Miss USA 2023 on September 29, 2023. 
  • She is an American-Venezuelan interior design student, model, and beauty pageant titleholder.

Noelia Voigt won the Miss USA 2022 pageant

Miss Utah Noelia Voigt has been crowned Miss USA 2023, beating out 50 other titleholders at the Pageant.

While Miss Hawaii Savannah Gankiewicz placed as first-runner-up and Miss Wisconsin Alexis Loomans has been second-runner-up.

The event was held at the Grand Sierra Resort in Reno, Nevada on September 29, 2023.

The judges were Vivica A. Fox, former “Real Housewife” Luann de Lesseps, and beauty vlogger Patrick Starrr.

Similarly, the crown was given to Voigt by Morgan Romano, who became Miss USA 2022 after R’Bonney Gabriel won Miss Universe.

How old is Noelia Voigt? Age, Parents, Education

Noelia Voigt was born to American father Jack David Voigt and Venezuelan mother Jackeline Coromoto Briceño on September 30, 2000.

Her full name is Noelia Victoria Voigt Briceño. Voigt was born in Sarasota, Florida, but raised in Park City, Utah. As of 2023, she is 23 years old.

Her father is a professional baseball player who played for del Zulia. Further, she had a younger sister named Neomy. Sadly, Neomy passed away just hours after birth due to complications.

In early September, she paid tribute to her beloved sister sharing a small clip of of her and her mother decorating Neomy’s memorial.

Talking about her academic qualifications, she received a Bachelor of Arts (BA) degree from the University of Alabama.

Noelia Voigt represented the state of Utah

She went to Salt Lake City, Utah, in April 2023 to compete for the title of Miss Utah USA 2023 after winning as first runner-up for the second time in a row at Miss Alabama USA.

In July, she was named the first Venezuelan-American named Miss Utah and ultimately Miss USA. She expressed her happiness with a sweet Instagram post,

“I am honored to be the first Venezuelan-American woman to hold the title of Miss Utah USA, and I am truly looking forward to breaking barriers and bridging the gap between communities by being able to speak Spanish and connect with the large Hispanic population in Utah,”

Noelia Voigt is the third woman from Utah to be crowned Miss USA after 63 years.

What’s next for Noelia Voigt?

During the Q&A session, Voigt was asked about her plans as a brand ambassador for the Miss Universe Organization and Miss USA.

In her response, she emphasized the fact that she is “bilingual Venezuelan-American” and expressed her commitment to connecting with diverse communities throughout the country.

The judges were impressed by her polished and thoughtful response, which played a vital role in her victory.

As Miss USA 2023, Voigt will represent her country well by engaging in philanthropic activities, advocating for causes, and competing for the Miss Universe crown.

The Miss Universe event is set to be held in El Salvador on November 18.

How tall is Noelia Voigt? Height, Profession

Her height is about 5 feet 7 inches tall, according to Wikipedia.

By profession, Noelia is a published author, professional lash technician, licensed esthetician, and workshop facilitator. She has also written a book named Maddie the BRAVE.

In addition, the newly crowned Miss USA is committed to empowering others with self-confidence through their versatile talents.

Is Noelia Voigt dating anyone?

Yes, Noelia Voigt is dating a guy named Jack Hendrix. Jack attended the same college where Voigt received her undergraduate degree.

The two have been dating for nearly five years. In October 2021, she shared an Instagram post with two adorable photos wishing her beau a happy third anniversary.

Jack is seen carrying her in the first picture of the post, while the second one shows him posing with a dog.

Similarly, she simply captioned the post, writing,

“happy 3 years my sweet! you make my (and mila’s) life a whole lot more fun and full of love. we love you so”
