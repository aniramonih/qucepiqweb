Is Bruno Mars Married? A Look at His Love Life and Relationships

Bruno Mars is not married and has never been married before. Even so, he is not single but has been in a long-standing relationship with Jessica Caban, an American model, actress, and fashion designer.

Bruno Mars is a famous American singer, songwriter, dancer, and record producer. He is known for his stage performances and various music styles, including rock, reggae, disco, R&B, soul, and funk. Away from his burgeoning professional life, many people have been wondering about the actual relationship status of the singer. So, in this article, we have explored everything there is to know about his love life, both past and present.

Does Bruno Mars Have a Wife?

Bruno Mars, whose real name is Peter Gene Hernandez, does not have a wife because he is not married. He also does not have an ex-wife because he has never been married before. Bruno Mars was born on the 8th of October 1985 and is presently 38 years old. His age and massive career has contributed to the reason many people simply conclude he is married.

But there is no truth in that, as the music boss is yet to walk down the aisle with any woman. Bruno has, however, been involved with several women in the past, including ones in the entertainment industry, and while he actually dated some of those women, some others were mere rumors and speculations.

Jessica Caban Has Been Bruno Mars’ Girlfriend For Over a Decade, But They Are Not Married

Bruno Mars has for over a decade been enjoying a peaceful and happy relationship with Jessica Caban, an American fashion model, actress, and fashion designer. Caban, 41, started her career as a model in 2003 after she was cast in Jennifer Lopez’s Sweetface collection.

In 2008, she was crowned the first-ever Model Latina champion. Jessica Caban made her acting debut in 2016 in the TV series Jane the Virgin. In the same year, she launched her own Swimwear brand. In addition to her hard work, Jessica’s relationship with Bruno Mars has, in many ways, helped her flourish in her diverse endeavors – business, modeling, and acting career.

She and Bruno Met at a New York City Hotel in 2011

Bruno Mars and her girlfriend, Jessica Caban, met for the first time in 2011 at CO-OP Restaurant & Bar in New York City, and it was love at first sight. At this time, Jessica was working as a dancer in the restaurant while Bruno was already famous in his singing career. Meanwhile, another source reports that the two first met at one of Bruno’s concerts or music video shoots.

Whichever is the case, the fact is that Mars and Caban fell in love the very first time they met. The singer asked Caban out after he was said to have followed her around in the restaurant for a while. Jessica accepted, and they started dating and have had a beautiful relationship to date. The duo has occasionally been seen out in public attending events together.

They also have been featured together in several magazines as a couple. Yet, they are described as notoriously private when it comes to information about their relationship; Bruno and Jessica try their best to keep details of their relationship and personal affairs reasonably away from the public eye and scrutiny.

They do not randomly post each other on social media apart from the periodic family group photos and birthday posts we occasionally see on the internet. Significantly, they have not courted any controversy, including infidelity rumors, ever since they came together. Regardless, On different occasions, the pair have publicly expressed that they are very much in love and enjoy spending time together.

In honor of their tenth anniversary in October 2022, Jessica shared a rare detail into their love life when she posted a throwback photo of herself and Bruno and captioned it, “Polaroid of our first trip together, 10+yrs ago”. The duo is very committed to each other in their relationship despite not having tied the knot yet.

Jessica has often said that she admires Bruno for his hard work and dedication to his career and how successful he has grown in music. She has also spoken highly of the love and bond they share a couple of times.

Jessica Caban Inspires Bruno’s Music

Beyond being in a romantic relationship, Bruno Mars has severally said that his girlfriend, Jessica, is the inspiration behind some of his biggest hits. Jessica’s influence can be heard and felt in some of his songs, like Just the Way You Are, Marry You, and Grenade. The lyrics of these songs and others are usually interpreted as a reflection of their relationship.

To say the least, Jessica Caban’s presence and influence in Bruno Mars’ life have been requisite to the success of his career. Bruno credits Jessica with the height he has reached in his life and music. She has been with him right from the time he was climbing the ladder of his music success. And today, they are enjoying an incredible life at the top.

Bruno Mars is a recipient of 15 Grammy Awards, four Guinness World Records, four Brit Awards, 14 Soul Train and 11 American Music Awards. His third studio album, 24K Magic, released in 2016, received seven Grammy Awards and won the major categories of Record of the Year, Song of the Year, and Album of the Year. Bruno has sold more than 130 million records across the globe, making him one of the best-selling music artists of all time.

Bruno and Jessica Do Not Have Children Together Yet

Despite being together for a long time, Jessica Caban and Bruno Mars do not share any children. In fact, none of them has a child of their own yet; this is quite distinct from the story of a typical celebrity romantic relationship where the couple sometimes are even together for the sake of their children.

Many people, especially their fans, have wondered the reason behind this. While some believe this is an intentional decision they made for themselves, others speculate that they’ve been trying to have kids. Whatever the case, the essential thing is that they are simply living their best lives together and, significantly, flourishing in their respective professional careers. They live together in Bruno Mars’ home in Los Angeles, California.

When Are Bruno Mars and Jessica Getting Married?

Fans and the general public have since been looking forward to hearing Bruno and Jessica break the news of their wedding date, but that has yet to happen. While they have been peacefully and happily together for about 12 years, Bruno and Jessica are not in a rush to walk down the aisle. Regardless, they are living nothing short of a married couple’s life.

The two love birds are simply taking their time to make decisions for themselves, and when they are ready, they will certainly share the news with their fans. Meanwhile, a few sources allege that the duo got married in a secret ceremony. We think this claim came about because Jessica has, in some of her interviews, referred to Bruno as her “husband.”

But, the truth is that they have not exchanged marital vows but recall that we said they are living nothing short of a married couple’s life and are deeply committed to each other. In one of his recent interviews with Rolling Stone, the multiple Grammy award-winning singer was asked if he would ever propose to his girlfriend Caban, and he answered, “Jesus! She’s my best friend. My rock. What’s wrong with that? …We’re just happy.“

Bruno Mars Relationships and Dating History Explored

For a young man who is not just successful in the entertainment industry but is also financially okay, it is one of the easiest things to understand that women would always be at his beck and call. There is no denying the fact that some of these women would even be the ones running after him.

For Bruno, the case is not different, although he manages to keep it low-key, unlike a few other highly successful and famous singers such as Chris Brown. In no particular order, below is a list of all the women Bruno Mars has dated or romantically linked with.

Rocsi Diaz

Rocsi Diaz is a 42-year-old Honduran-born American television host, radio personality, and model. She and Bruno Mars were rumored to be dating sometime in 2011. The news of their relationship circulated all over the internet and was birthed after they were spotted shopping in Paris and Milan while holding hands together.

During this period, Bruno had been on a tour and allegedly spent most of his time with Rocsi. Moreover, this period happened to coincide with Bruno’s birthday and the alleged couple spent it together and had quite a good time. After that, they also attended public events a couple more times, sparking more dating rumors.

However, to the disappointment of their fans, who loved them together, neither Rocsi Diaz nor Mars ever confirmed their relationship, and it eventually turned out to be mere speculation.

Rita Ora

Rita Ora is a 33-year-old British singer and songwriter popularly known for featuring in DJ Fresh’s 2012 hit single Hot Right Now. Ora and Mars reportedly dated between 2009 and 2001. The duo first met at one of Bruno’s concerts in London and at that time, Rita Ora was only 18 while Bruno was 24, and both of them were not yet famous in the industry.

However, their relationship did not stand the test of time and they broke up sometime in 2011, with Ora claiming that their age gap stood as a challenge for them, whereas Bruno claimed it was the long distance between them that strained their relationship. Regardless, since after their split, Rita and Bruno have remained good friends, with Rita admitting that she is thankful for the times they spent together.

She has often mentioned that Mars inspired and gave her massive support towards the growth of her music career. She even revealed that Bruno was her first love. Meanwhile, Rita Ora got married to New Zealand filmmaker Taika Waititi in August 2022, and they have been together since then.

Chanel Malvar

Chanel Malvar is a music video model and professional backup dancer. She and Bruno met when she danced at one of Bruno’s tours. Their relationship was, however, on and off again between 2009 and 2011, and within this period, both of them collaborated on many projects and were part of Bruno’s Hooligans band.

They tried to keep their relationship away from the public and it was only found out they had split in 2011. Chanel toured many places performing at sold-out concerts with Bruno and also featured in some of his music videos. Their relationship ended quietly and without any controversy, and while they have since moved on; they still remained friends.

Amelle Berrabah

The relationship between Bruno Mars and Amelle Berrabah, a 39-year-old British singer and songwriter, seems to be only a mere rumor. The two were presumed to have dated sometime in 2011. They met at a music event and thereafter were spotted a couple of more times together at events.

However, after a few weeks, they weren’t seen together again. And, as the story goes, neither Amelle nor Bruno addressed the dating rumors. Meanwhile, Amelle Berrabah married Marcio Sousa Rosa in 2014 and in 2020, they got divorced.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Miley Cyrus Married To Bruno Mars?

Miley Cyrus and Mars are not married. In fact, have never been in a romantic relationship. Cyrus is a 53-year-old American singer, songwriter, and actress. The two are just iconic figures in the pop music scene and have together graced several red carpets and attended high-profile award shows.

Is Bruno Mars Married To Jessica Caban?

Bruno Mars is not yet married to his girlfriend, Jessica Caban. The two are in a longterm relationship and fans are expectant that they will eventually get married and that their love remain forever after.

Is Bruno Mars Married To Nathalie Kelley?

Nathalie Kelley and Bruno Mars are not married. As a matter of fact, there is no proof that shows that the Peruvian-Australian actress and the Just the Way You Are crooner were ever in a romantic relationship. Kelley married Jordy Burrows in 2018 and in 2020, they separated.
