Dubai Bling TV Star Danya Mohamed (Diva Dee) Age, Birthday, Zodiac Sign, Parents, Net Worth

Dubai Bling is a Netflix reality show that follows the glamorous lives of self-made millionaires in Dubai.

One of the cast members who has caught the attention of many viewers is Danya Mohamed, also known as Diva Dee.

She is the wife of famous DJ and producer Marwan Al-Awadhi, aka DJ Bliss, and a successful vlogger, entrepreneur, and social media star.

But who is Danya Mohamed, and what is her story?

In this article, I will reveal everything you need to know about the Dubai Bling actor, including her age, birthday, zodiac sign, parents, net worth, and more.

Danya Mohamed Age

Danya Mohamed was born on March 31, 1983, in Russia.

Danya Mohamed is currently 40 years old as of 2024.

Danya Mohamed Nationality

Danya Mohamed is of British descent but she has not disclosed much information about her family background.

She moved to Dubai at a young age and considers herself a proud Emirati.

Danya Mohamed zodiac sign

According to her birthday, Danya Mohamed’s zodiac sign is Aries.

Aries are known for being adventurous, energetic, confident, and passionate.

They are also natural leaders who like to take charge and initiate new projects.

Danya Mohamed certainly exhibits these traits, as she is always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow and learn.

She is also very ambitious and driven, as she has achieved a lot in her personal and professional life.

Danya Mohamed Parents and Family

Danya Mohamed is married to DJ Bliss, a hugely popular and influential DJ in the Middle East.

They got married in 2017, having a grand Islamic wedding ceremony that Danya shared on her Instagram account.

They are blessed with two adorable children, a son named Zayed and a daughter named Meera.

Danya loves being a hands-on mom, often sharing cute pictures and videos of her family on her social media.

While Danya hasn’t shared much about her own parents, she mentioned that they support her career choices and lifestyle. She also appreciates the warm and loving welcome she receives from her husband’s family, who treat her like their own daughter.

Danya holds her family close and considers them her greatest blessing.

Danya Mohamed Net Worth

Danya Mohamed’s estimated net worth is around $1 million, as of 2023.

She earned this sum from various income streams, including her YouTube channel, Diva Dee.

On this channel, she shares vlogs and videos about makeup and beauty, boasting over 118k subscribers and more than 24 million views.

Her influence extends to Instagram, where she has over 126k followers, promoting diverse brands such as Bambini Fashions, Mahallati Jewellery, Gucci, and Gaffe Studios.

Danya Mohamed Career

Beyond content creation, Danya Mohamed wears the hat of an entrepreneur.

She manages several businesses, including BYD, a streetwear cap line, and The Bicnic, an event-organizing company.

Further expanding her entrepreneurial ventures, she is actively involved in the process of opening a coffee shop and investing in apparel, skincare, and technology companies.

Embracing a philosophy of crafting unique and innovative concepts, she seeks avenues that generate substantial revenue with minimal investment.

Conclusion

Danya Mohamed is not just a content creator and entrepreneur; she is also recognized as a TV personality.

Prior to her participation in Dubai Bling, she appeared on various shows. Currently, she stands as one of the main cast members of the Netflix reality show, providing a glimpse into her opulent lifestyle in Dubai.

Notably, she holds the distinction of being the first Emirati female featured on a Netflix reality show, thereby earning recognition as a trailblazer and role model for women globally.
