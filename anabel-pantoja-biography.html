Anabel Pantoja Bio (Wiki)

Who Is Anabel Pantoja?

Anabel Pantoja aka Ana Isabel Pantoja Bernal is a well-known Spanish tv and Instagram star who is known for her appearance in the media group, Mediaset Espania, which was produced by La Fábrica de la tele.

Apart from this, she has also gained popularity as the niece of Isabel Pantoja, who is a Spanish singer.

Anabel Pantoja- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

The Spanish star Anabel Pantoja was born on July 15, 1986, in Seville, Spain. As of 2022, she is 36 years old and holds Spanish nationality.

Likewise, her ethnicity is European and as per the astrologers, her birth sign is Cancer.

Moving on to her family background, she was brought up in an artist family. Her aunt, Isabel Pantoja is a Spanish singer while her uncle Agustin Pantoja is also a singer.

Education

As per Anabel Pantoja academic, she went to Madrid to study se formó como maquilladora, convirtiéndose en la asistente de su tía. Later, she was trained as a makeup artist, and also as the assistant for her aunt.

Anabel Pantoja- Professional Life, Careers

In 2011, Anabel Pantoja began her television career as a collaborator on El programa de Ana Rosa on Telecinco.

From 2013 to 2016, she appeared in the program Mujeres y hombres y vice-versa along with Telecinco. She signed with Supervivientes, in 2014 as a contestant but was expelled after fifteen days.

Collabing with ¡Qué tiempo tan feliz!, from 2015 to 2017, and was contestant in Jesus Vazquez on Levántate All Stars.

Prior to joining the television program, Sálvame, with sporadic collaboration, participated in the Gran Hermano 16 debate in 2015.

After participating in the program, she was able to accumulate a large following among the public, especially on social media. She garnered a million followers on her Instagram account.

Anabel joined Ei tiemp del descuento but was expelled, and later became a contestant in El tiempo del descuento, in which she reached the final. Followng that, she joined f Solos/Solas  in September 2020.

Apart from being a tv star, Pantoja started her entrepreneurial career. In the initial, she opened a nail business and worked as a designer of a swimwear collection as well as the jewelry brand Leuli.

Adding up more, she then rose to fame as a social media influencer. Famous Korean singer of the group, Loona picked her up after seeing her dancing video.

Afterward, she began designing accessories in 2021, and participated in the Portuguese artist Nininho Vaz Maia’s video clip, as a protagonist.

Talking about her most recent project, in the summer of 2021, she starred in the short Telencio documentary titled, Anabel, al desnudo.
