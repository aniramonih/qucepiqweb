Frank Buckley Biography | Wiki

Frank Buckley is an American anchor and reporter. The Award-winning broadcast journalist currently serves as an anchor at KTLA. The podcast also streams live on his Youtube.

As a reporter, Frank got a chance to visit every city in Southern California. He has also been to different locations all over the world.
