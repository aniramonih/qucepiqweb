How Tall Is Daniil Medvedev?

Russian professional tennis player Daniil Medvedev is presently ranked eighth in the world. His unconventional playing style and lanky 6’6″ physique have made him well-known.

In 2019, after making it to the U.S. Open final and losing to Rafael Nadal in a thrilling five-set showdown, Medvedev made his maiden appearance in the top 10.

Since then, his excellent baseline technique and aggressive approach to short balls have helped him establish himself as one of the top players in men’s tennis.

Medvedev controls points from the back of the court with his long reach and flat groundstrokes. He is extremely proficient at the net and serves.

How Tall Is Daniil Medvedev?

Daniil Medvedev stands at a height of 6 feet 6 inches or 193m tall. He also weighs about 83kg.
