Dadju Djuna Nsungula (French pronunciation: ​[dadʒu dʒuna ɛnsunɡula] ), better known by the mononym Dadju or at times Prince Dadju (born 2 May 1991 in Bobigny, Seine-Saint-Denis, France), is a French singer. He was signed to the label Wati B and in 2017 signed with Polydor Records of Universal Music Group. He was a member of the musical formation Shin Sekaï alongside rapper Abou Tall from 2012 to 2016. The duo was part of the label Wati B and released the album Indéfini. The duo announced that they were breaking up so they can each follow solo careers. Dadju released his solo album Gentleman 2.0 in 2017.
