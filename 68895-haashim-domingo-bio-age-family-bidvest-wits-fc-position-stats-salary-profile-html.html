Haashim Domingo bio: age, family, Bidvest Wits F.C., position, stats, salary, profile

Haashim Domingo has a promising career in the South African Absa premier league. The attacking midfielder will be joining Mamelodi Sundowns at the start of the 2020/2021 season for a five-year contract. This comes after his current team, Bidvest Wits Football Club, sold its top-flight status to Tshakhuma Tsha Madzivhandila (TTM) and had to let go of its top players.

Haashim, just like other excelling soccer players, has had a long journey to become the one he is today. His passion for football started when he was only a toddler. He later joined the Ajax Cape Town Academy, where he learned the skills he needs to play football. He played across Europe before returning to South Africa to join the Bidvest Wits F.C.

Early life and family

Haashim Domingo Wits was born on 13th August 1995 in Mountainview, Cape Town, South Africa. He comes from a humble background. He is a religious person and believes that what he has achieved is because of Allah’s will. The soccer player prefers to keep his private life out of the public limelight.

Career

Haashim is a talented soccer player that was nurtured in the Ajax Cape Town Academy. He started playing professional football in 2015 for Vitoria Guimaraes B and scored a goal in a game against Atletico CP on 14th February 2015. He played for the Portuguese team until 2018. He also played for Pevidem SC and Raufoss on loan in 2016.

He later joined Bidvest Wits Football Club, a team in the Absa Premiership, on 2nd August 2018 on a free transfer. Bidvest Wits F.C. will sell its top-flight status to TTM (Tshakhuma Tsha Madzivhandila) at the end of the 2019/2020 Premier Soccer League season. TTM boss, Masala Mulaudzi, confirmed that it will not manage to pay the salaries of most of the high-earning Wits players including Domingo, Thulani Hlatshwayo, Monare, Deon Hotto, Buhle, and others. Top South African teams, including Orlando Pirates, Kaizer Chiefs, and Mamelodi Sundowns are ready to purchase the players.

Is Haashima Domingo signed to Mamelodi Sundowns?
