Otto Waalkes Overview and Wiki

Early life

Born on July 22, 1948, in the serene town of Emden, East Frisia, Germany, Otto Gerhard Waalkes’ early life was marked by simplicity and a strong family bond.

The second son of Karl Waalkes, a master painter, and his wife Adele (born Lüpkes), Otto’s upbringing in the working-class district of Transvaal provided a grounded foundation for his future endeavors. This modest beginning in a close-knit community played a crucial role in shaping Otto’s down-to-earth persona and relatable humor.
