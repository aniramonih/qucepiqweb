Nicola Peltz (Actress) Biography, Age, Height, Weight, Net Worth, Husband, Net Worth, Facts

Nicola Peltz is an American actress born on January 9, 1995, in Rye (State of New York). She was revealed with the role of Katara in the film The Last Airbender but she is better known for the role of Bradley Martin in the television series Bates Motel and for that of Tessa Yeager in the film Transformers: The Age of extinction.

Nicola Peltz was born in Rye, Westchester County, New York. Her mother, Claudia, is a former model and her father, Nelson Peltz, is a businessman. She has four older brothers, two younger brothers and a sister, one of her brothers is actor Will Peltz. We can also mention Diesel Peltz as her big brother.

Nicola Peltz began her career in 2006 with a role in the film Neighbor against neighbor then in 2008, she played the role of Becki in the film Harold by T. Sean Shannon.

She made herself known to the general public in 2010 by interpreting Katara in the film The Last Airbender, the film adaptation of the animated series Avatar, the last airbender from Nickelodeon.
