He was the Former United States Secretary of Labor. Furthermore, the 72-year-old author served in the presence of former Presidents Gerald Ford, Jimmy Carter, and Bill Clinton. In 2006, Robert appointed as a Professor of Public Policy in Goldman School of Public Policy at UC Berkeley.

In his career, he has published 18 books. It includes “The Work of Nations”, “Reason”, “Saving Capitalism”, “Supercapitalism”, and many other successful books. In fact, his best-selling e-book is Beyond Outrage.

The 72-year-old professor founded a media company named “Inequality Media” in 2013. In addition, his partner is Jacob Kornbluth. The company produces interviews & videos on social media.
