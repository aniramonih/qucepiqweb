Who Is Seth Rogen?

Seth Rogen was born on April 15, 1982 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. He is a Canadian-American of Jewish origin who started stand-up comedy as a teenager and made his way into the movie industry as an actor, director, voice artist, and producer.

Seth earned critical appraise for his performance in the film The 40-Year-Old Virgin. In coming years, he received a breakthrough from the films Knocked Up and Funny People. His other major films included The Green Hornet, Sausage Party and Neighbors.
