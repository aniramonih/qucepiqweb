Born Jennifer Lynn Lopez to David Lopez and Guadalupe Rodriquez who hail from Puerto Rico she is the second child among three siblings. She left home to Manhattan after disagreeing with her parents, who were forcing her to pursue further her education in college, yet she wanted to be a movie star.
