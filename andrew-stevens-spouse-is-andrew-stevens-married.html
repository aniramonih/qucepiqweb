Andrew Stevens Spouse: Is Andrew Stevens married?

Herman Andrew Stevens, an American executive, producer, director, and actor, was born on June 10, 1955.
The only child of actress Stella Stevens and her former husband Noble Herman Stephens, Stevens was born in Memphis, Tennessee. Stevens has worked as a writer, director, and actor in addition to producing.

He first made an appearance in Shampoo (1975) in a small role. He then went on to appear in cult thrillers including Massacre at Central High (1976), Vigilante Force (1976), Day of the Animals (1977), and the legendary horror movie The Fury (1978) starring Kirk Douglas.
