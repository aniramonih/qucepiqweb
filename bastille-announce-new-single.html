Bastille Announce New Single - CelebMix

After almost three years, Bastille has finally announced a new single! The track is titled “Good Grief” and is set to come out next Thursday, June 16.

The news comes just one week after the announcement of their second album, Wild World. 

Good evening you lot. We're massively excited to announce our second album "WILD WORLD" https://t.co/pZqLWEFTki

— BASTILLE (@bastille) June 2, 2016

Wild World has no set release date but a video linked to the album announcement has a teaser for a new song that fans are over the moon for.
