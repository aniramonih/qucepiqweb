Tyler Williams Bio (Wiki)

Who is Tyler Williams?

Tyler Williams is an American Youtuber and businessman. His self-titled Youtube channel has more than 1.2 million subscribers.

Moreover, he is the CEO and founder of Nextbeat, a digital space allowing for the creation of interactive vlogging experiences.

Tyler Williams: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Childhood, Ethnicity

Tyler Williams was born on May 30, 1990, in the United States of America. His birth sign is Gemini. As of 2022, his age is 31. He has not revealed much about his early life and family background.

There is no information about his parents. Similarly, he has not revealed anything about his siblings.

He loved sports like swimming and water polo from a young age. Moreover, he also loved singing. His ethnicity is Caucasian.

In which school did Tyler Williams attend?

Talking about his education, he went to ‘The Haverford School’. At Haverford, he played sports such as water polo and swimming for 4 years. Then, he learned Latin and Chinese at ‘Haverford’ and was a member of its choir.

Following that, Tyler Williams majored in economics at ‘Stanford University.’ There, he continued his passion for singing. He also performed with Stanford’s cappella group, ‘Fleet Street Singers’.

Tyler Williams: Professional Life, Career

After earning his graduation from Stanford, he landed the job of corporate analyst of finance at ‘The Walt Disney Co.’ in Burbank.

After working there for more than a year and a half, he served as ‘Disney’s global business analyst for 10 months. Following that, he laid the foundation of the application named ‘Bubl, Inc.’ in L.A. in November 2014.

He and two software engineers from Stanford started working to develop a media app. They aimed to develop an app that would allow its users to create videos and photos within a temporary social circuit that would exhaust after a day.

The app was upgraded to a live video media platform that allowed its users to share experiences in real-time.

In July 2015, he launched ‘Nextbeat’ which is an interactive digital space to create interactive vlogs and videos in real-time.

On December 6, 2016, he started his self-titled Youtube channel. He started off with a series of ‘Try It Yourself’ videos where he created things he had never made before. The series became popular and gained a lot of viewers and later it started featuring food tasting videos.

Some of his videos are hugely popular. His video titled, ‘We Stayed In A Japanese Capsule Hotel’ uploaded in 2019 has more than 12.7 million views.

Some of his other popular videos include ‘We Made Transparent Potato Chips’ (8.9 million views), ‘I Tried A Sushi “Bazooka” From Amazon’ (3.5 million views), ‘I Swapped Clothes With My Girlfriend For A Week’ (3.3 million views), etc.

As of May 2020, his channel has more than 1.2 million subscribers and his total video views have crossed 61.7 million views.
