Jack Degnan Dobbs Ferry and jack degnan obituary

The New York community is grieving the loss of 63-year-old Jack Degnan, a longtime resident of Irvington, NY who passed away this week. Jack was a loving family man and dedicated Account Executive at Essity in Westchester County. His sudden death leaves his wife, children and many friends beyond devastated.

Early Life and Education

A native of Dobbs Ferry, NY, Jack Degnan attended Archbishop Stepinac High School and graduated in 1978. He went on to earn a B.A. in English Literature from Manhattan College in 1982.

Jack's academic achievements laid the foundations for a successful career in sales and marketing over the next four decades.

Professional Accomplishments

Degnan built an accomplished career in the publishing and paper product industries. He worked as Production Director at Ziff Davis Enterprise from 1989-2007 before becoming a Sales Rep at Edward Don & Company in NYC.

Most recently, Jack served as District Sales Manager at Wausau Paper for 7 years prior to joining Essity in Westchester County as an Account Executive. He was regarded as talented and dedicated in each role.

Beloved Family Man

While Jack thrived professionally, his true joy came from family life. He was a devoted husband to wife Noreen for over 37 years and caring father to their children Kevin, Sean and Kelly.

Friends recall Jack's endless love and pride for his family. As “Popa Degs,” he embraced every moment with his grandchildren too. Jack's kindness and humor uplifted those around him.

Shock and Disbelief

As word of Jack's untimely passing spreads, friends and neighbors express profound grief and disbelief that he is gone. The Irvington community valued Jack's warm spirit, generosity and engaging personality.

Many recall his brave act in 2013 when he risked his life to save a fallen student from oncoming trains. This exemplified his selfless character. Jack made Irvington brighter and will be deeply missed.

Legacy of Positive Impact

While his loss leaves a void, Jack Degnan's legacy shines on through all the lives he positively impacted over 63 years - from family to colleagues to neighbors.

Jack lived life authentically with compassion, humor and service to others at the forefront. Though gone too soon, his spirit remains through those fortunate to have known him.
