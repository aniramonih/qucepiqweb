Ally Kane Obituary, Funeral Notice

A memorial for Ally Kane, Catherine
On Friday, June 23, 2023, quietly at the Ottawa Hospital Civic Campus At the early age of 23, Ally went away surrounded by her devoted family.

Beverly and Dennis Kane’s daughter Ally was adored by everyone. sister of Kyle (Stacey), who is very loved. cherished Tasha with a particular fondness. Granddaughter of Bernardine Murphy, the late Leo, the late Donald and Elsie Kane, and Bernardine Murphy.

Dear niece of Maureen Murphy (Randy), Wayne Murphy (Betty), Kathy Millar (Jeff), and Karl Murphy (Bev), as well as Monica Kane-Baggio (Rick), Bruce Kane (Debbie), Paula Kane-Perry (Jaimie), and others.

Ally was a superb athlete who excelled in every sport in which she took part. She was stunning on the inside and out. The room would shine with the smile of Ally. She was such a kind soul who loved her family and friends with all of her heart. All those who had the honor of knowing her will miss her for the rest of their lives.
