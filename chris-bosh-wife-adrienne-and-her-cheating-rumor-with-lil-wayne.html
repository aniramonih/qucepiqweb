Chris Bosh Wife Adrienne And Her Cheating Rumor With Lil Wayne

Like the former NBA star, Chris Bosh’s wife, Adrienne Bosh, has also seen significant success in her own undertakings. 

Adrienne Bosh is an entrepreneur, a philanthropist, and a mother of five. She’s also gotten a lot of media attention due to being Chris Bosh’s wife.

In the past twelve years, the Bosh family has built a life together filled with love, joy, passion, and respect.

Even though the life of Boshs looks perfect on the outside, they have had rough days as well.

Chris Bosh is a former professional basketball player who played in the NBA. He made an indelible mark on the basketball world during his NBA stint.

Bosh was born on March 24, 1984, in Dallas, Texas, to Noel and Freida Bosh. Standing at 6 feet 11 inches tall, he showcased his talent and versatility throughout his career. 

Bosh became “Texas Mr. Basketball” in high school. His collegiate career was limited to a single season at Georgia Tech.

Bosh was selected fourth overall by the Toronto Raptors in the 2003 NBA draft. He played as a power forward and center, earning 11 NBA All-Star selections. 

His later team, Miami Heat, retired his jersey “No.1” in honor of the two times NBA champion.

Chris Bosh Wife: Adrienne Bosh

Adrienne Bosh is very well known as the wife of former NBA star Chris Bosh. She is an actor, model, entrepreneur and philanthropist.

They got married on July 19, 2011. The couple has four children together, while Chris also has a daughter with his ex-girlfriend.

She was born on April 17, 1985, as the daughter of Susan Chatham and Rogelio Vargas. The Vevay, Indiana native did her schooling at Switzerland County High School.

After winning her first beauty contest, she ventured to Los Angeles at the age of 17, following her passion for modeling.

Luckily, Adrienne found success in acting and secured roles in movies like “Three Barbeques,” “Martin Luther King Day,” and “How To Throw A Party.” 

Along with guest roles in several television shows, she also appeared in numerous music videos, photoshoots, magazines, and advertisements. 

Aside from entertainment, Adrienne began her entrepreneurial journey by establishing Diamonds & Dimes Entertainment. It is a club promotion business in Ohio.

Further, she founded Sparkle & Shine Darling boutique store. They cater to women’s needs and provide unique shopping experiences.

The philanthropic ventures of the successful businesswoman are also abundant, which extend to various community service efforts.

Adrienne founded a mentorship program called Vice President of Team Tomorrow Inc. in Miami. Similarly, she funded various organizations and charities. 

In addition, Adrienne founded the Chris Bosh Foundation together with her husband. The foundation focuses on promoting sports and education among youths.

Adrienne And Her Cheating Rumor With Lil Wayne

In 2013, Lil Wayne dropped a bombshell at an NBA All-Star Weekend event when he claimed that he slept with Chris Bosh’s wife Adrienne.

The rapper went on a vicious rant and went off on the NBA, the Miami Heat, and the Heat’s Big Three at the time LeBron James, Dwyane Wade, and Chris Bosh.

Wayne was kicked out of a Miami Heat / L.A. Lakers game in Miami a week prior for allegedly cheering for the Lakers. 

Adrienne responded to the accusation on Twitter, where she retweeted inspirational posts in an effort to clear her name.

However, Lil Wayne was not making false accusations whimsically. Adrienne reportedly had a fling with Wayne prior to marrying Chris.

It was also rumored that Adrienne asked Lil Wayne for financial support, citing her cancer treatment.

Furthermore, the rumor also said that Wayne gave her money only to never hear from her again.
