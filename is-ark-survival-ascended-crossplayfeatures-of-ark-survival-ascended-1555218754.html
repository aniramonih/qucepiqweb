Is Ark: Survival Ascended Crossplay? Features of Ark: Survival Ascended

Discover seamless gaming adventures with Ark: Survival Ascended! Unleash the excitement of crossplay on Xbox, PlayStation, and PC. Join forces, explore, and survive together across platforms. 

Is Ark: Survival Ascended Crossplay?

Yes, Ark: Survival Ascended supports crossplay, allowing players on Xbox Series X|S, PlayStation 5, and PC to join forces in the game. However, there's a temporary hold on crossplay between PC and consoles, mainly due to a delay in the console release. Once the game is available on consoles, crossplay will be activated.

Moreover, the game offers cross-platform progression, a feature that lets players seamlessly transfer their items and progress across different gaming platforms. This ensures that players won't lose their hard-earned achievements if they decide to switch to another platform. Many players appreciate this feature as it enables them to continue their in-game adventures without starting from scratch when changing platforms.

Additionally, ARK: Survival Ascended introduces cross-platform modding, thanks to Overwolf. While PC players have traditionally enjoyed modding for an enhanced gaming experience, console players were limited to the base game and DLCs. With the arrival of ARK: Survival Ascended, console players can now access and enjoy mods created by PC players, unlocking a new realm of possibilities and customization for players across all platforms.

Ark: Survival Ascended

Ark: Survival Ascended is an action-adventure survival video game created by Studio Wildcard. Released initially on Windows in October 2023, with later releases planned for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series S/X, it serves as a revamped version of the 2015 game Ark: Survival Evolved.

Set in a prehistoric world filled with dinosaurs and other creatures, players must survive, tame wildlife, build shelters, and cooperate or compete with other players. The game's early access period on Windows allows players to experience and contribute to its development. With enhanced features and improvements, Ark: Survival Ascended aims to provide an immersive and challenging gaming experience, building on the foundation of its predecessor.

Features Of Ark: Survival Ascended

Ark: Survival Ascended comes with several exciting features and improvements:

  • Unreal Engine 5 Overhaul: The game has been upgraded with advanced technologies like Nanite, Lumen, and NVIDIA's RTXDI, enhancing graphics and performance.

  • Cross-Platform Multiplayer: Players on PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series S/X can join forces, exploring and surviving together.

  • Cross-Platform Modding: Enjoy user-created content across all platforms, thanks to Overwolf.

  • New Content and Dynamic Water: Expect fresh gameplay experiences, including dynamic water effects using FluidNinja.

  • Interactive Foliage and Cutscenes: The environment comes to life with interactive foliage, and cutscenes add cinematic elements to the gaming experience.

  • Performance Gains: Unreal Engine 5 improvements bring performance gains, such as instanced rendering for large bases and threaded server networking.

  • Dynamic Navigation Mesh and Creature Pathfinding Overhaul: Creatures navigate more intelligently through a dynamic navigation mesh.

  • Cross-Platform Anti-Cheat: A system is in place to maintain fair play across all platforms.

  • Quality of Life Improvements: Enjoy enhanced gameplay with features like an updated mini-map, queueing system, third-person camera, directional sprinting, ping system, and gamepad cursor.

  • Chibi Slot, Skins Tab, and DLSS Support: Customize your experience with new slots, skins, and NVIDIA DLSS support.

  • Photo Mode: Capture and share your in-game moments with the new photo mode.

  • Dino/Baby Management QoL: Improvements in managing wild babies and dinosaurs.

  • Snap Point Improvements: Logic improvements and new snap points enhance building structures.

  • Character Creation & Customization Improvements: Enhancements for creating and customizing your in-game character.

  • Tek Binoculars Rework and Updated Control Scheme: Improved functionality for Tek Binoculars and an updated control scheme on controllers.

  • Gameplay Changes: Significant changes include electrical generators powering appliances wirelessly, a simplified irrigation system, and the removal of the Speed Status on official servers.

  • Wild Babies: Occasionally, wild babies can be found with their parents and can be claimed by slaying or taming the parents.

    • These features collectively contribute to a more immersive and enjoyable gaming experience in Ark: Survival Ascended.
