Who is Blake Corum’s Girlfriend, Makiah Shipp?

Makiah Shipp is the current girlfriend of Blake Corum. Born on August 21, 2002, Makiah resides in Detroit, Michigan, with her family. From as far back as she can remember, Makiah has harbored a deep passion for social justice. Her outstanding efforts earned recognition from the city of Detroit at an early age. Makiah is actively involved with organizations such as the Rhonda Walker Foundation and Midnight Golf. Additionally, she is the author of a children’s book titled ‘Makiah’s Show and Tell’ and contributes as a political analyst, podcaster, and writer for the Black Student Union’s Spectrum magazine.
