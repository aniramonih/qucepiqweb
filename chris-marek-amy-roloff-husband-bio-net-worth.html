Is He Still Married to Amy Roloff?

Chris Marek and Amy first met at a singles mixer at Vine Gogh Artist Bar & Studio in Portland in 2016. It was the same period she finalized her divorce from Matt Roloff. At first, they were just friends but later on, Chris invited Amy for a motorcycle ride and asked her out.
