Ellen Adarna is a Filipino actress, former internet celebrity, and model who is best known for portraying roles such as Clarize in My Illegal Wife, Racquel in You’re Still the One, Isadora “Issa” Sobrevista in Langit Lupa, and Dalikmata in Indio. She has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Alice Bungisngis and her Wonder Walis, Pasión De Amor, Home Sweetie Home, Wildflower, Tween Academy: Class of 2012, Beauty in a Bottle, and many more. She was a contestant on Survivor Philippines: Celebrity Doubles Showdown in 2011. As a model, Ellen has been featured in magazines like FHM, Women’s Health, Esquire, and UNO.

Boyfriend / Spouse

Ellen Adarna has dated –

  • J.C. Intal (2010) – Ellen Adarna was in a relationship with basketball star J.C. Intal in 2010. However, their relationship was fairly brief and lasted for about 6 months only. Also, their breakup seemed to have been messy as well. In her interviews, she recalled her time with Intal as the worst six months of her life.
  • Filo Cucueco (2010-2013) – After the end of her relationship with Intal, Ellen hooked up with businessman Filo Cucueco. In 2011, she managed to convince him to join her on the reality TV show, Survivor Philippines Celebrity Doubles Showdown. She had warned him that if he didn’t participate along with her, she would find a proxy boyfriend for the show. Even after the end of the show, they dated for about two years before calling it quits in 2013.
  • Raoul Olbes (2012-2014) – Ellen reportedly started going out with Raoul Olbes, who ran his family’s business empire, in 2012. They were close friends for about five years before they decided to give their relationship a try. She eventually released a statement confirming that they were in a relationship and praised him for not being insecure. They were also living together but it didn’t last long as they broke up in September 2014. In her statement, she revealed that they had issues, which they found hard to resolve.
  • Diether Ocampo (2013) – Ellen was rumored to be dating actor Diether Ocampo in 2013 but the speculations were never confirmed. However, in her October 2013 interview, Ellen revealed that Ocampo had asked her out. But, it isn’t likely that she took his proposal seriously.
  • Sebastian “Baste” Duterte (2016) – Adarna started going out with TV host Sebastian “Baste” Duterte, the son of the Philippines’ then-president, in the summer of 2016. Their relationship is said to have lasted until the end of the year. In her January 2017 interview, she confirmed that she had dated him in the past.
  • John Lloyd Cruz (2017-2019) – Ellen had started dating actor, model, and TV host  John Lloyd Cruz in 2017. They have a son together named Elias (b. June 2018). The couple had parted ways in 2019.
  • Derek Ramsay – Ellen got married to model and actor Derek Ramsay on November 11, 2021.
