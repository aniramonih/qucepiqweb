The Crew Motorfest Release Time, Date, Early Access, Features, Gameplay &amp; More

After four long years of waiting, racing enthusiasts are gearing up for the thrilling launch of The Crew Motorfest, Ubisoft Ivory Rower’s latest installment. While the general release date is fast approaching, we’ve got the inside track on how you can hit the asphalt ahead of everyone else.

Launch Date: Get Set, Go!

Ubisoft is set to release The Crew Motorfest on 14th September 2023. Fans across various platforms are in for a treat as the game will be available on:

  • PC (through both Ubisoft and Epic stores)
  • PlayStation 4
  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One
  • Xbox Series X|S

For the majority, the 14th of September is the date to mark in their calendars. However, for those eager to jump the gun, there’s a way to start your engines earlier.

Accelerate Your Gaming Experience: Early Access

Avid gamers can slide into the driver’s seat from the 11th of September 2023, a full three days before the official launch. To avail this early bird offer, one needs to purchase one of the two exclusive digital-only versions of the game:

  • Gold Edition
  • Ultimate Edition

Both editions, available only for digital purchase on PC, PlayStation, and Xbox platforms, offer this three-day head start. Do note: the physical-only Limited Edition does not offer this perk.

Moreover, PC users subscribed to Ubisoft+ game library service can play the Ultimate Edition of The Crew Motorfest from the 11th. However, the ‘Multi Access’ version, which encompasses select titles on Xbox or Amazon Luna, does not cover The Crew Motorfest.

What’s Under the Hood: Editions Breakdown

Gold Edition (£84.99/$99.99) Features:

  • Cross-gen versions for PS4 and PS5 or Xbox One and Series X|S (excluding PC)
  • A Year 1 Pass unlocking 25 vehicles over the upcoming year
  • The crucial three-day early access

Ultimate Edition (£84.88/$119.99) Offers:

  • Cross-gen versions for PS4 and PS5 or Xbox One and Series X|S (PC excluded)
  • Year 1 Pass (same as the Gold Edition)
  • Three-day early access
  • Additional packs including Fitted Ultimate, Fitted Rainbow, and Fitted Jungle Packs

Pre-load and Play

For those who’ve opted for the Gold or Ultimate Editions, Ubisoft offers a pre-load option. This means you can download the game before the 11th, ensuring you hit the road the moment it unlocks.

Pre-loading Dates for Gold and Ultimate Versions:

  • PC (Ubisoft Connect) – From 9 am BST / 10 am CEST on 9th September
  • PS4 and PS5 – From 9 am BST / 10 am CEST on 9th September
  • Xbox One and Xbox Series X|S – From 8th September 2023

Standard Digital Edition Pre-loading Dates (Play starts 14th):

  • PC (Ubisoft Connect) – From 5 pm PDT / 8 pm EDT on 11th September to 1 am BST / 2 am CEST on 12th September
  • PS4 and PS5 – Same as PC
  • Xbox One and Xbox Series X|S – From 8th September 2023

The Early Bird Races First

To sum it up, for early access to The Crew Motorfest, mark the date: 11th September, available exclusively to Gold or Ultimate Edition purchasers.

As anticipation for The Crew Motorfest revs up, fans worldwide are preparing to immerse themselves in Ubisoft’s open-world racing magic. Whether you’re jumping in on the 11th or the 14th, one thing’s for sure: The Crew Motorfest promises to be a wild ride.
