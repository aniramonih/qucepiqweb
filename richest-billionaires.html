What is Karen Lo's Net Worth?

Karen Lo is a Hong Kong heiress who has a net worth of $1 billion. Karen Lo is best known for being a billionaire heiress to the Vitasoy beverage empire. Vitasoy was founded in 1940 by Dr. Lo Kwee-seong. The Hong Kong beverage company sells beverage and desserts. It first produced a high-protein soy milk drink but has expanded to other beverages including fruit juice, tea, milk, water, soft drinks, and tofu.
