What is Cara Horgan's Net Worth?

Cara Horgan is an English actress who has a net worth of $500 thousand dollars. Cara Horgan was born in South East England. She studied at the Drama Centre in London before making her film debut in 2004's "The Libertine". She followed that up with the part of Claire Newman in 2005's "The Rotters' Club" and Veronica Vass in "The Afterlife". Horgan played Mary Shelley in 2006's "The Romantics", Eliza Reed in "Jane Eyre", Joanna Clifford in "Fallen Angel", and Anna Holland in "Silent Witness". She went on to appear in "Comedy Showcase: Ladies and Gentlemen", "Peep Show", "The Boy in the Striped Pyjamas", "Lewis", and "Waking The Dead".

Horgan's more recent film credits include the parts of Colin's secretary in "My Week with Marilyn", Elizabeth Lerner in "Law and Order UK", and Roxy "The Wedding Video". Horgan is also an accomplished stage actress, playing Hedda Gabler at the Gate Theatre in 2008. A year later, she scored the part of Irene in "Pains of Youth" and, in 2010, she appeared in the Bristol Old Vic production of "Far Away". Her other stage appearances include "The School for Scandal" at the Barbican Centre. As of 2013, Horgan became part of the Secret Theatre Company.
