Jennifer Hudson’s ex-fiancé, David Otunga and their son, David Daniel Otunga, Jr. looked dapper in tuxedos as they ushered in wrestling’s biggest event over the weekend.

In the midst of the Coronavirus threat, wrestling fans had the rare treat of witnessing this year’s WrestleMania 36 from the comforts of their homes. The pre-taped event was aired this weekend over WWE Network’s streaming service.

David, a professional wrestler himself, made sure to alert fans of the weekend’s highlight. He shared a photo of him and his son at a WrestleMania outing and wrote,

“#wrestlemania weekend begins tonight! The current state of things provides an opportunity for unique matches and increased creativity, which will no doubt make #wrestlemania36 a memorable one!”

The photo David shared featured a stadium with an audience that tells us it wasn’t a new photo. This year’s WrestleManila was pre-taped on March 25 and 26 at the WWE Performance Center training facility in Orlando without an audience.
