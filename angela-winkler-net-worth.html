FAQs About Angela Winkler

Who is Angela Winkler?

Angela Winkler is a renowned German stage and screen actress known for her versatile roles in both European cinema and theatre. She has worked with many celebrated directors and is noted for her deep, nuanced performances​.

What are some of her most notable films?

Some of her most significant roles are in films like The Tin Drum (1979), The Lost Honor of Katharina Blum (1975), Benny’s Video (1992), and Clouds of Sils Maria (2014).

Has she won any awards for her acting?

Yes, she has received several accolades, including a Film Independent Spirit Award. She is also celebrated in her home country with awards like the German Film Awards.

What was her breakthrough role?

Her breakthrough came with the film The Lost Honor of Katharina Blum in 1975, which made her a star in Germany and garnered international attention.

Has she worked in any international projects?

Yes, she has appeared in international projects, including Dark (2017), the first German-language Netflix original series, and the remake of Suspiria (2018) directed by Luca Guadagnino.

What are her hobbies or personal interests?

She enjoys performing at events and has been involved in cabaret performances, such as at Cabaret Phoneyisland in Berlin in 2009.

Does she have any family?

She lives with her partner, sculptor Wiegand Wittig, and has four children. Her daughter Nele, who has Down’s syndrome, is also an actress and part of the ensemble at RambaZamba theatre in Berlin.
