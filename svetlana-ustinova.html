Svetlana Ustinova

MOSCOW – JUNE 19: Ustinova, 36th Moscow International Film Festival. Opening Ceremony at Pushkinsky Cinema. June 19, 2014 in Moscow, Russia, magicinfoto/Bigstock.com

Birth Name: Svetlana Vladimirovna Ustinova

Place of Birth: Severodvinsk, Arkhangelsk Oblast, Russian SFSR (now Russia)

Date of Birth: 1 May, 1982

Ethnicity: Russian

Svetlana Ustinova is a Russian actress.

She is the daughter of Tatyana and Vladimir Ustinov. Svetlana was previously married to Ukrainian film director Mark Gorobets.
