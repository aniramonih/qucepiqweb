Davido Net Worth, Career, Early Life, Family, Top Songs, Awards and Achievemnets

David Adedeji Adeleke, widely recognized as Davido, is a prominent figure in the international music scene.  He is not just an entertainer but also a skilled songwriter and record producer.

His net worth has led to numerous rankings, often positioning him as one of the youngest and most successful artists in Africa. Do you happen to know Davido’s current net worth?

Who is Davido?

Davido, born on November 21, 1992, in Atlanta, Georgia, United States, moved to Nigeria with his family during his childhood.

His parents are the late Mrs. Vero Adeleke, a professor, and Dr. Deji Adeleke, a prosperous Nigerian entrepreneur with various business ventures.

Davido’s high school years took place at the British International School in Lagos.

For his university education, he initially attended Oakwood University in Alabama, United States, studying Business Administration.

However, he chose to leave and focus on his music passion. Later, at his father’s request, he enrolled in a music degree program at Babcock University in Ilishan-Remo, Ogun State.

In 2015, he successfully earned a Bachelor of Arts Second Class Upper degree.

What is Davido Net Worth?

Davido’s net worth is $25 million, primarily amassed from his music career.

He’s also widely recognized in Africa and secured a significant $5 million contract as an MTN communication brand ambassador, as reported by local African newspapers.

Career

In 2011, Adeleke launched his music career with the release of his hit single, “Dami Duro,” the second single from his debut album, “Omo Baba Olowo.”

This song marked a turning point in his career, receiving acclaim not only in Nigeria but also internationally.

Since 2011, Davido has made significant strides in the music industry, producing several albums and singles.

He’s also engaged in numerous musical collaborations with both local and international renowned artists.

His exceptional talent, determination, and hard work have led to numerous award nominations and over 20 wins in various musical categories.

Davido’s Family

Davido highly values his family. He grew up with his mother, Vero Adeleke, and his father, Adedeji Adeleke, a Nigerian billionaire businessman.

He also has two older siblings, Adewale Adeleke and Sharon Adeleke.

Davido is a father to four children. He has two daughters, Imade and Hailey, with his ex-girlfriend Sophia Momodu.

With his wife Chioma Rowland, he has two sons, Ifeanyi (who passed away in 2022) and David Jr. Recently, Davido and Chioma celebrated the arrival of twins, a boy and a girl.
