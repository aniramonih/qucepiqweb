What is Fiona Geminder's Net Worth?

Fiona Geminder is an Australian businesswoman who has a net worth of $2 billion. Fiona Geminder is the youngest daughter of the packaging tycoon Richard Pratt. Geminder owns 40% of Pact Group which is a plastic packaging business with her husband Raphael Geminder. The company went public on the Australian Securities Exchange in 2013.

Fiona Geminder also owns a stake in the family paper packaging and recycling business Visy with her billionaire brother Anthony Pratt and billionaire sister Heloise Waislitz. Fiona Geminder graduated from Monash University. She lives in Melbourne, Australia and is married with four children.
