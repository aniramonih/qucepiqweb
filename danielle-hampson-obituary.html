Danielle Hampson Obituary: When Was The Funeral Held?

Danielle Hampson obituary details were not revealed by her family. 

The unexpected and heartbreaking death of PR Executive Danielle Hampson on the morning of her wedding day, Saturday, 18th June, deeply impacted all those close to her, including her beloved fiancé, Tom Mann. 

In tribute to his late fianceé, Tom took to social media with a photo of family and friends gathered together in a park for a memorial service. 

The caption accompanying the post expressed his devastation with simply two words: “Today was for our Dani” followed by a single white heart emoji. Despite their grievous loss, it is clear that Danielle will live on in the hearts and minds of those who held her dear.

Tom, who rose to fame as a member of the Stereo Kicks band on the OTV shows the X factor, also shared a photo of himself kissing Danielle in front of a lake. 

In a touching Instagram post, he announced her death, stating that he was completely broken trying to process the tragedy. 

He went on to say that he would do everything in his power to raise their son, Bowie, and to make sure he knew just how amazing his mother was.

Danielle’s father, Martin Hampson, also honored her memory by sharing a photo of Danielle smiling with Bowie on his personal Instagram account. 

He wrote that Danielle was taken from them on what should have been one of the highlights of her life and that the family was struggling to come to terms with the loss.

Danielle’s cause of death has not been made public. However, her passing has elicited a huge outpouring of condolences and thoughts from many high-profile individuals, including Lewis Capaldi, Ellie Goulding, and Emma Bunton of the Spice Girls.

Danielle was not only a PR Executive but also a dancer. She appeared in Harry Styles’ 2021 music video for “Treat People With Kindness” and also danced alongside the Spice Girls and Take That. 

Dani and Tom had originally planned to get married in 2020, but the coronavirus pandemic forced them to postpone the nuptials.
