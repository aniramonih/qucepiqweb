Quinton Cable Viewing, Obituary and Funeral c Cane Bay Assist. Baseball Coach

Quinton On Sunday, March 5, 2023, Michael Cable, 29, of Summerville, passed away.

Visitation will take place at James A. Dyal Memorial Home in Summerville on Friday, March 10, 2023 from 10:00 am to 11:30 am. On March 10, 2023, at 11:30 a.m., a funeral service will start in the chapel at James A. Dyal Funeral Home, 303 S. Main St., Summerville.

Following, there will be a burial at the Wassamassaw Baptist Church Graveyard.
The son of Michael and Linda Cable, Quinton was born on December 28, 1993 in North Charleston, South Carolina. In addition to his family and friends, he adored his Lord. He received his degree from Clemson University.

For Cane Bay High School, Spartanburg Methodist, and Florence-Darlington, he was a softball player. He was passionate about teaching.

He was a kind-hearted young man who is dearly loved by his parents and everyone who had the privilege of getting to know him. He is profoundly and painfully missed.

He is also left by his parents, his uncle Jerry Hayes (Mary), and his cousins Bryce and Ryan Hayes (Raegan).
You can leave the family a note by going to our website at www.jamesadyal.com.
