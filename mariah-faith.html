American Idol Season 21 Contestant Mariah Faith Wiki | Bio

Mariah Faith (age: 22 years; born on January 11, 2001) is an aspiring singer from Baltimore, Maryland, who recently came to the limelight after appearing as a contestant in season 23 of “American Idol.”

Upon reaching the stage, Mariah introduced herself and told about her background. During the introduction, she made everyone emotional by speaking about her stepfather’s contribution to her life.

She sang Vince Gill’s “Whenever You Come Around” very beautifully, and it was clear from the judges’ faces how much they liked her performance. Katy Perry said, “I just feel like there is a lion that needs to be let loose in there,” and then she told her to sing Bonnie Raitt’s “I Can’t Make You love me.”

She sang the second song so well that her performance brought tears to the eyes of Luke Bryan; Lionel Richie was also very impressed with Faith’s singing and said, “What we just heard is just the tip of the iceberg, but your potential could take you seriously all the way.” In the end, Mariah received the Golden Ticket and moved to the Hollywood Round.

How Old is Mariah Faith?

On January 11, 2001, Mariah Faith (age 22) was born to her mother, Trina Michelle Cornwell, in Baltimore, Maryland, America. Not much is known about her biological father, but we learned she had a stepdad named Brian, Big B. Mariah is the middle child and has two sisters: Miranda Drocella and Marina Love Cornwell.

At 9, the Baltimore native moved to a small town Conway where she studied at Carolina Forest High School and participated in various singing competitions. Following High School, in 2019, she enrolled at Academy for Technology and Academics for higher studies.

Support of Stepfather

As we said earlier, Mariah spent the first 8 years of her life in Baltimore and then moved to Conway, South Carolina. The reason behind coming to the newer small town was to live with her stepdad, who supported the “American Idol” contestant in pursuing her dreams and becoming a trained singer.

Faith gives full credit for her excellence in singing to Brian and never misses to mention how her wonderful folk supported her.

Sadly on April 21, 2019, Big B went out to buy a gift for his princess and, on the way, suffered a heart attack and left the world. Brian’s demise left the family heartbroken. Mariah is now on a mission to establish herself in the American music industry and fulfill the dream of her stepfather.
