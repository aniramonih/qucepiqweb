Sally Rand's About

Sally Rand was born on April 3, 1904 (age 74) in Elkton, Missouri, United States. She is a Celebrity Dancer. Burlesque performer and film actress known by the stage name of "Billie Beck." She was famous for her trademark "bubble dance" and "fan dance" and was the star of the 1933 Chicago World's Fair. According to numerology, Sally Rand's Life Path Number is 3. Her the best movies are Sunset Murder Case (1938), Bolero (1934), A Girl in Every Port (1928), Braveheart (1925) and The Fighting Eagle (1927). Her education: Columbia College. She died on August 31, 1979, Glendora, CA. Her spouse is Clarence Robbins (?–?) Thurkel Greenough (1941–?) Harry Finkelstein (1949–1950) Fred Lalla (1954–1960).
