Know about Swamp People Star Chase Landry's Life as a Father, Ex-wife, Children and Ex-girlfri

The Landry trio, Troy, Jacob, and Chase, have made a career out of alligator hunting as the family together navigate across the challenging and dangerous swamps of Louisiana to hunt alligators.

The American reality series based on the day-to-day life of alligator hunters first premiered on History Channel in 2010. Since then, there have been 13 seasons of the reality adventure television series that span 60 minutes.

As of now, fans have been awaiting to watch their favorite hunters in action in the upcoming season 15 of the reality series. 

Amongst the hunters, Chase Landry, one of the best sharpshooters in the Louisiana swamps, manages a very private life. He is not active on any social media platforms either.

Read the full article to know about Chase Landry's life as a father, ex-wife, children, and ex-girlfriend Cheyenne Wheat

How many Children does Chase Landry have?

The alligator hunter has always frayed away from publicly disclosing his personal life.

However, there was a major revelation made by Chase’s father, Troy Landry, through Facebook on  8 March 2017, which revealed that he had become a father.

In the post, Troy shared a couple of adorable pictures of Chase’s daughter named Riley Blake Landry.

Troy Landry reveals his son Chase Landry's daughter. (Photo: Troy Landry's Facebook)

"Congratulations to Chase, his wife, and the whole Landry family," one fan commented.

Another chimed, "You look just like a proud grandpa. congrats."

And while Troy made sure to update fans about the birth of Riley, Chase, on the other hand, has not disclosed any information about who his child's mother is.

Is Chase Landry's Ex-Wife the Mother of His Daughter?

After the birth of Chase's daughter, many fans showed curiosity to know the actual mother of his baby, or his wife, so to say.

According to The Cinemaholic, Chase Landry was reportedly married to Chelsea Kinsey and together, they gave birth to Riley.

The two separated not long after the birth of their daughter.

Did Chase Landry get Married to Cheyenne Wheat, a.k.a Pickle?

After his relationship with Chelsea Kinsey was over, he started dating his Swamp People co-star, Cheyenne Pickle Wheat, who first appeared in season 12.

The pair not only bonded on a romantic level but also over their mutual interest in hunting and passion for wildlife.

Fans saw the pair achieve several achievements in the reality show.

Cheyenne’s Facebook also has a few photos of her hanging out with Chase. More importantly, she changed her relationship status to 'In a Relationship with Chase Landry' on 25 October 2020.

Are Chase Landry and Pickle still Dating?

Unfortunately, Chase and Pickle are no longer dating.

Within a year of their relationship starting, most of the posts that were related to him were removed from her account.

In early 2022, Pickle's Facebook showcased her "single" status. However, the reason for their breakup is still unknown.

Chase Landry and Cheyenne Wheat posing after a hunt on October 26, 2020. (Photo: Cheyenne Wheat's Facebook)

That being said, she is still close to Chase's father, Troy, and usually posts pictures with him on Instagram and Facebook. 

As for Chase and Pickle's current relationship, they share a friendly bond and can be seen enjoying each other's company while hunting in the reality television series.

Pickle is now Dating and has a newborn daughter

Cheyenne Pickle Wheat, a.k.a Pickle, has been dating Joshua Kippes, an emergency medical technician. 

The pair welcomed a daughter on April 27. 

On International Women’s Day in March 2023, Pickle had shared a heartfelt caption about her pregnancy:

You aren’t here yet but, this day was made for you and many magnificent women who have came before. I hope and pray that you see the world as I do and add to it along your way. A rain drop on a rose pedal born from a stalk of thorns… My Maemi Rae.

Previously, she and her partner had announced her pregnancy on Facebook for the first time, sharing a picture of them holding copies of her ultrasound scan.

Chase’s Career Is Closely Tied with His Family

American alligator hunter Chase is more than lucky to be surrounded by people who love him not just at home but also at work.

He is a well-known face of the History Channel’s reality TV show, Swamp People, which follows a group of people living off-catching alligators in the Atchafalaya River basins of Louisiana.

Chase is also one of the sons of Troy, a veteran alligator hunter.

Chase, along with his brothers Jacob and Brandon, follow their father's footsteps, all of whom, together, reveal the Cajun way of life in Louisiana in the hit TV show. 

With his brother Jacob and father, he is often seen rowing their boat into the murky waters, searching for the best alligators to pass the hunting season.

Chase has weathered lawsuits, bruises, and mishaps, but his family has always remained beside the reality star. 

While he is often seen on television sharing the most crucial part of his hunting passion with his brothers, cousin, and father, fans rarely see him sharing about his conjugal life.

What is Chase Landry's Net Worth?

According to Celebritynetworth, Landry has an estimated net worth of $200 thousand, whereas, his father Troy has an estimated net worth of $2 million.
