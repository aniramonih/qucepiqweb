Premier League club, Everton manager Carlo Ancelotti is married to his wife, Mariann Barrena McClay

If you are a football fan and are crazy about football then this next personality is no stranger to you. Carlo Ancelotti is a former Italian professional football player who is currently managing Premier League giant, Everton. He is one of the three managers in the world who has won three UEFA Champions League titles once with Real Madrid and twice with Milan. 

Sixty-one years old, Ancelotti is was born in Reggiolo, Italy. He and his siblings were raised by working-class parents in Italy. Having an early interest in football, he soon pursued his career as a football player and has played clubs like Parma, Roma, and Milan where he has won numerous titles and awards. 

Moreover, till now, he has managed clubs like Reggiana, Parma, Juventus, Milan, Chelsea, PSG, Napoli, Everton, Bayern Munich and Real Madrid. 

Exchanged wedding vows with wife, Mariann Barrena McClay

Successful football coach, Carlo has impressed the world with his unique style of managing players. He often gets into highlight with his tactics and winning mentality. Talking about his relationship status, he is a married man. He exchanged his wedding vows with his longtime girlfriend, Barrena McClay. 

According to the couple, they started dating each other back in 2011 and have been united since then. With several drinks and dates, they decided to take their relationship one step further. In July 2014 the beautiful pair accepted one another as husband and wife. The particular ceremony was held in Vancouver with numerous football legends were seen to congratulate the newly married couple. 

As of now, they are married for seven years and seem very much blessed with one another. During this seven years nuptial journey the duo shares one daughter, Katia Ancelotti. Despite being a busy personality, Mr. and Mrs. Ancelotti are still taking good care of their daughter. 

Previous failed marriage and affairs

Before accepting McClay as his wife, Ancelotti was previously married to his first wife, Luisa Gibellini. Memorizing their old days the couple exchanged their wedding vows back in 1982, at that period of time, Carlo was coaching the Italian giants, Milan. However, after enjoying twenty-five years long nuptial life the couple quit their relationship and start living together. Their divorce was finalized in 2008 which was publicly announced by the coach. 

Despite the ended their twenty-five years long martial journey, they are still parents on one son, Davide Ancelotti. Davide is playing professional football following his father's footsteps. He married his wife, Ana Galocha with whom he shares twins children, Lucas and Leon. As of now, both of Carlo's grandsons are two years old respectively. Furthermore, he previously dated his Romanian girlfriend, Marina Cretu for a couple of years. 

Carlo Ancelotti net worth - $50 Million

Football legend, Carlo enjoys a wonderful net worth of $50 Million as of 2021. He has accumulated all of his fortunes through his footballing career as a player and coach. He is one of the successful football managers in the world with an impressive net worth and salary. Currently as a coach of Premier League giant, Everton he is earning a salary of $10.5 Million annually. 

Moreover, he has numerous houses in both Italy and England which prices are over $3 Million in today's market. Another football legend and coach, Pep Guardiola who is managing EPL giants, Manchester City has a net worth of $40 Million as of 2021.
