How Old Do You Have to Be to Work at CVS?

CVS Health is a pharmacy chain with over 7,000+ CVS locations around the US. It aims to provide good quality personal healthcare by offering more accessible and affordable services for individuals and local communities.

How Old Do You Have To Be To Work at CVS?

To be employed at CVS, you must be aged 16 years or older. You will need to meet this age requirement if you want to work for an entry-level role or any other position at this company. Regardless of the role you’re applying for; you’ll be part of an inspiring and inclusive team working towards providing a unique approach to healthcare.

Can you work at CVs if you’re 15?

No, to be able to apply for a job at CVS, you must be aged 16 years and older.

Can you work at CVs if you’re 16?

Yes, you can apply to work at CVS if you are 16 or older, as this is the minimum age you’ll need to be to apply!

Are There Any Special Requirements to Work at CVS?

Some jobs require you to be over the age of 16, and you may be required to have certificates for some roles. Please search the CVS Health website for the latest accurate information on specific job requirements before applying.

What Can CVS Offer You?

The company has many career opportunities for both experienced and entry-level applicants. They have a unique workforce initiative that includes partner teams of other agencies to provide employment and training to the wider under-represented community.

CVS also ensures that it provides a fulfilling work experience for its team. This allows its workers to promote the company vision to provide high-quality service and to transform healthcare.

What Are The Benefits of Working at CVS?

CVS offers a Total Rewards scheme that provides competitive pay, paid time off, and other employee benefits such as medical, dental, eye care, and prescription health care that are flexible and suited to individual needs.

It also offers support to help you set up a Health Savings account (HSA) and can help provide accident and disability benefits.

You’ll also receive employee discounts at CVS Pharmacy stores, along with the My-Well Being program which offers a holistic care program to ensure all employees have access to health services.

CVS also provides employee resources across six central dimensions, which include a focus on physical health, social connectedness, and financial security.

Popular Teen Jobs at CVS and What to Expect

There are many opportunities to work at CVS if you are 16 years and older. CVS is an entry-level friendly company that provides training and opportunity for professional development. So here are some of the options available…

Apprentice Pharmacy Technician

Training to become a pharmacist technician is a popular entry-level role at CVS. And due to the number of pharmacies nationwide, there are always opportunities to apply.

CVS have also introduced a Registered Apprenticeship Program to promote low-income job seekers to apply for roles and kickstart their careers.

Hear more about the interview process for a trained pharmacy technician’s role by checking out this Youtube Video.

Job responsibilities and skills required:

To apply for this entry-level role, you must be aged 16 years or older. Applicants should also preferably have a national pharmacy technician certificate which is either the PTCE or the ExCPT. However, training is available for those who have not passed the test.

You need to have a willingness to learn in a training environment and be able to assist the pharmacists in their roles. Also, having good knowledge of customer service requirements is useful and being able to communicate well with others is necessary.

CVS pharmacy technicians usually complete prescriptions for customers, so you will be trained to become familiar with this process. As a trained technician, you will also be responsible for operating the cash register, calling up customers, and collecting payments for prescriptions.

Trained technicians usually work between 20-40 hours full and part-time.

To find out more information on this job role at CVS, please visit the CVS Health website or consult the hiring manager at your local store when applying or during the interview.

Store Associate

This is an important customer-focused role where you will be supporting customers with their needs by answering questions about products or services or resolving any issues. To apply, you must be aged 16 years or older.

For more info about the interview process, take a look at this Youtube video.

Job responsibilities and skills required:

Some experience in a customer service or retail background is desirable with an ability to work well both independently and as part of a team. You’ll also need to be good at communicating, and be friendly and welcoming to customers.

Another essential duty is handling cash, card transactions, and operating the tills, and you must make sure to follow strict procedures. You will also be responsible for restocking shelves, completing inventory management tasks, and manning the shop floor.

Other duties include assisting the store manager or pharmacy personnel with tasks.

Shift Supervisor/Management Trainee

This job is to train and prepare an employee for a higher-level role. In order to be effective at this, you’ll have to complete both basic and management operations training and fully understand the nature of CVS as a business.

Job responsibilities and skills required:

To work as a shift supervisor trainee at CVS, you must be at least 16 years old and have your high school diploma or GED. You’ll also need to have good leadership and communication skills in order to support other employees throughout the training and be responsible for their performance.

Experience in a customer service or retail environment is essential because you’ll be working with customers and the store management team. You’ll also need to assist and support customers when required, answering any questions or solving issues or complaints whilst following strict company policy.

How Much Does CVS Pay?

The exact rate of pay varies depending on your experience and the stores’ location, but there is a general guideline on the salary you can expect when working at CVS.

Sales Associate

A sales associate should expect to earn an average starting salary of around $10, depending on your experience.

However, experienced sales associates are offered discounts on products or merchandise, and there are opportunities to progress to managerial roles within the company.

Shift Supervisor/ Management Trainee

As you go through management training, you will have the opportunity to grow in the company and, therefore, earn more. The average wage for a shift supervisor/management trainee is around $14 an hour.

Apprentice Pharmacy Technician

For this entry-level role, the average salary is $8-$10 an hour. However, experienced, certified, or trained Pharmacy Technicians expect to be paid between $11 and $14 an hour.
