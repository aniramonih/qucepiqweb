When is Hearthstone Battlegrounds Season 3 Going To Begin?

Hearthstone Battlegrounds Season 3, which will introduce a new minion type, the return of a “warped hero,” and some Silvermoon and Scourge-based cosmetics will begin on January 17th Blizzard has revealed.

The developer has announced that Hearthstone Battlegrounds will receive 32 new minions for season 3, including 16 new undead ones and 11 new dual-type ones. The official site notes that “although the Undead focus around death effects like Reborn and Deathrattle, Dual-type minions have synergies with two different minion classes at once.

Hearthstone Battlegrounds Season 3 also sees the return of Professor Putricide, who now has a Hero Power called “Build an Undead” that allows you to create three unique undead as you play.

The remaining primary official details for Hearthstone Battlegrounds Season 3 are as follows:

On January 17, Battlegrounds Season 2 will end, and Season 3 will begin. At that point, players will switch over and begin earning rewards on the new Season 3 Battlegrounds Track. Any rewards that were already earned from the Season 2 Battlegrounds Track but not yet claimed will be automatically claimed.

The Battle for Silvermoon has spilled into Battlegrounds for Season 3! This Battlegrounds Track includes chilling new Scourge-themed, and fiery new Silvermoon-themed, cosmetics. Pick up the Season Pass to instantly get +2 Hero options at the start of each game, unlock all stages of the new Silvermoon Legendary Strike along the Track, and earn up to 15 additional Hero Skins throughout the Season.

Blizzard has promised to unveil more about Season 3 over the next few days, with teases of minion card reveals, a new hero reveals, a balance update and developer interview, and more interviews leading up to the weekend. On January 12th, from 9 am PT (5 pm UK) until 3 pm PT (11 pm UK), teams from all around the world will compete in a sneak peek of Season 3 for the chance to win 15 season passes for themselves.
