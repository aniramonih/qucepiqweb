Billy Warlock Biography, Age, Wife, Baywatch, Happy Days &amp; Net Worth

BIOGRAPHIE DE BILLY WARLOCK

Billy Warlock is an American actor. He is best known for playing Eddie Kramer, a lifeguard on the first three seasons of Baywatch and in the 2003 reunion movie. He is also known for many daytime roles, including Frankie Brady on Days of Our Lives and AJ Quartermaine on General Hospital.

BILLY WARLOCK AGE

Billy was born on March 26, 1961 in Gardena, California. He is 57 years old in 2019

FAMILLE BILLY WARLOCK

Billy was born William Alan Leming in Gardena, California. He is the son of Dick Warlock, a successful Hollywood stuntman who did stunts for Kurt Russell and was the stunt driver in Disney’s picture, The Love Bug. He has two siblings Lance Warlock and one sister, Rhonda.

He graduated from Birmingham High School in Van Nuys, California in 1979. His father landed him a stunt job as Robin Williams’ voice actor on an episode of Mork & Mindy, produced by Garry Marshall. Several months later, Billy auditioned for Marshall and landed his first major television role as Flip, Roger’s brother (and the Cunninghams’ nephew), on the tenth season of Happy Days.

WIFE OF BILLY WARLOCK | BILLY WARLOCK AND JULIE PINSON

Billy is currently married to former Days of our Lives and As the World Turns co-star Julie Pinson. The couple tied the knot on August 26, 2006, in Las Vegas, Nevada after rekindling their romance while working on set together!. Their ceremony was an intimate affair, with the bride wearing the original Vera Wang ivory duchess satin gown she had purchased for their first engagement, seven years earlier! The groom wore a black Canali suit.

BILLY WARLOCK BAYWATCH

In 1989, Billy appeared as an original cast member of Baywatch. He played lifeguard Eddie Kramer for the first three seasons. He also participated in the short-lived television series The Hat Squad in the early 1990s, with Nestor Serrano and Don Michael Paul from 1992 to 1993, and portrayed Lyle Menendez in the 1994 TV movie Honor Thy Father and Mother: The True Story of the Menendez Murders. Billy reprized his role as Eddie Kramer in the television movie Baywatch: Hawaiian Wedding in 2003.

BILLY WARLOCK RETURNS TO DAYS

Billy went on to star in soap operas such as Days of Our Lives and General Hospital. In Days of Our Lives, he portrayed Frankie Brady from 1986 to 1988, from 1990 to 1991, and again from June 2005. Billy won a Daytime Emmy Award in 1988 for Outstanding Younger Actor in a Drama Series for his performance in the series. Billy was released from the soap and its last air date was November 2006.
