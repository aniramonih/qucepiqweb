Early Life

Shawn Elizabeth Killinger was born on the 2nd of November 1979 in Michigan, United States. She grew up in Grosse Pointe and Detroit until 11 years old when her life changed dramatically. Her father got a job opportunity in Mexico City, and the whole family packed their bags and moved to a new country. Shawn was nervous and excited at the same time. She had to learn a new language, a new culture, and a new way of life. She enrolled in the International American School Foundation, where she met other kids from different backgrounds and countries. She quickly became fluent in Spanish and Mexican, and developed a passion for travel and learning.

Shawn returned to the US with her family when she was 15 years old. She missed Mexico, but she was also happy to be back in her homeland. She continued her education in United States. After completing high school she joined Penn State College where she graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing. Done with her education, Killinger traveled around Europe for quite a while.

Professional Career

Shawn Killinger is a versatile and charismatic TV personality who has been in the media industry for over 25 years. She started her career as a production assistant at CBS News in 1995, where she learned the ropes of broadcast journalism. She was over two-year with the network and worked on CBS 60 Minutes and CBS Evening News With Dan Rather. But the highlight of her stay is undoubtedly her work on the Late Show With David Letterman. She then moved on to become a morning reporter and fill-in anchor at WRGB-TV CBS in Albany, New York, where she covered breaking news, politics, and entertainment for three years. She then joined WKMG- TV as a morning anchor in Orlando. Shawn got her much-awaited break when she was chosen to feature in the reality TV game show entitled The Apprentice: Martha Stewart. The show aired on the NBC network and while Shawn was sent home in the third week, she managed to make the most out of her time. She got a call from QVC two years later and has been working for them as the host of a four-hour-long live broadcast.

Here is a quick timeline of Shawn Killinger’s career:

  • 1995: She graduated from Penn State University with a B.S. in marketing.
  • 1995-1996: Interned at CBS News on the CBS Evening News with Dan Rather and The Late Show with David Letterman
