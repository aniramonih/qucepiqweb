Chris Evans Finally Gave aHumble Reply to Lizzos Drunk DM to Him on Instagram

Lizzo just posted a video on TikTok with Chris Evans' sweetest reply after drunkenly falling into his DMs a few days back.

The singer shared a screenshot of her Instagram DMs to the Captain America star on Saturday (17 April), in which she sent him three emojis of a puff of wind, a sports player, and a basketball.

The two were not following each other on Instagram at that point. 'The Good as Hell' singer lip-synced to audio from TikTok user Tatayanna Mitchell while the message played in the background. 

She elaborated that the reason she’s sad about this one is that she knows she won’t be able to marry him, and it really hurts her. “Because damn papa, he a rare breed, no comparing.”, she added.

“Don’t drink and DM, kids,” Lizzo captioned the post “For legal porpoises, this is a joke.”

Evans Has Replied to Lizzo’s Drunk Instagram DM

But now, the singer is more excited than ever because Evans has finally replied to Lizzo’s drunk Instagram message. 

She shared a screenshot of Evan’s response to her 14million TikTok fandom. His reply read, “No shame in a Drunk DM” with a kiss emoji. The actor also jokingly said that he has done worse on the app.

Not only that, it looks like Lizzo’s drunken DM has gained her a new follower. It’s none other than the man himself; Evans now follows the 'Juice' singer on Instagram. 

The clip has over 4 million views and 1 million likes in just a few hours. The comment section was flooded with thousands of comments as fans were as surprised as she was. One user commented, “She’s living all our dreams.” Another person wrote, “I need this to happen, I’m so invested y’all”. A third user commented, “You’ve won at life”. 

People also want to know what will happen next as one of the top liked comments read, "You must keep us updated."
