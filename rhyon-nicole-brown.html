Rhyon Nicole Brown - Bio, Age, Height, Net Worth, Facts

Rhyon Nicole Brown is a multi-talented actress, singer, and dancer from the United States. Rhyon Nicole Brown has been active in the entertainment industry since 1999 and appeared in various roles in TV series and movies. One of her notable roles is playing Elizabeth “Lizzie” Sutton in the TV series “Lincoln Heights,” which aired from 2007 to 2009.

Early Life

Rhyon Nicole Brown was born in Los Angeles, California, the United States, on the 6th of October 1992. Currently, she is 31 years old and has a zodiac sign of Libra. She holds US nationality and belongs to the African-American ethnicity.

The identities of her parents are Josh Brolin and Alice Adair. She grew up in Los Angeles with her two sisters, one of her sisters’ names is RaVaughn, a singer by profession.

Rhyon Nicole Brown – Education

In terms of her academics, she is a graduate of Junipero Serra High School in San Juan Capistrano, California. In addition, she graduated from the University of Southern California.

Professional Career

In 1999, Rhyon Nicole Brown made her acting debut starring as Julia in the Christmas film “Santa and Pete.” During the same period, she made her television debut, playing the recurring role of Rebecca Van Exel from seasons 1-5 of “Judging Amy” (1999-2004). In 2000, she appeared in three TV series “Providence,” “The Geena Davis Show,” and “ER” appearing in an episode in TV series.

Moreover, she played the recurring role of Amanda Timmerman from 2002 to 2003 in the TV series “Hidden Hills.” At first, she guest starred as Madison in season 2 of “That’s So Raven” and her role was praised by the audience. As a result, she got a recurring role in the series season 3.

Rose to fame

She boosted her acting career when she played the role of Elizabeth “Lizzie” Sutton in the TV series “Lincoln Heights,” which aired from 2007 to 2009. In 2015, she appeared in a recurring cast as Pam in season 4 of the TV series “Tyler Perry’s For Better or Worse.” She played the recurring role of Maya in season 4 of the TV series “Empire.” Later, she was appointed as the main cast in the series seasons 5 and 6.

Movies Works

Apart from her television work, she has done movies. She appeared in a crucial role in some Hollywood movies such as “Get Rich or Die Tryin” (2005), “Throwback Holiday” (2018), and “Killers Anonymous” (2019).

Moreover, she also appeared in a crucial role in some Popular Hollywood television movies such as “Surviving Compton: Dre, Suge & Michel’le” (2016), “Earthquake” (2004), and “A Christmas Stray” (2021). She appeared as a lead actress in a Hallmark television movie named “The Spring Breakthrough.”
