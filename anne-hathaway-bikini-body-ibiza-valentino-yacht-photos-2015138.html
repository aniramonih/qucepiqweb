Anne Hathaway Rocks Two String Bikinis on Valentino's Yacht: Photos

Talk about living your best life! Anne Hathaway and her husband, Adam Shulman, spent Aug. 12 and 13 sunning aboard Valentino‘s yacht off the coast of Ibiza, Spain. So naturally, the Oscar-winning actress packed a couple of cute bikinis for the occasion.

On Wednesday, Hathaway, 32, modeled her trim physique in a palm-print, triangle-top two-piece by Mara Hoffman, topped by a sheer Lemlem caftan. She accessorized the swim ensemble with navy-blue sunnies and a charm necklace. A slicked-back ‘do and minimal makeup (save for a pale pink manicure!) completed the vacation style.

The next day, she rocked a swimsuit just as flattering. The star of the upcoming The Intern (with Robert De Niro) chose a teeny white and blue-printed string bikini, which she wore while splashing in the sea before cuddling with her husband of almost three years on the boat, owned by the Italian fashion icon, Hathaway’s longtime friend.

While the Princess Diaries alum has yet to share any personal pics from her vaca, she did take to Instagram at the start of the week to post an inspiring quote from Dr. Seuss: “Be who you are and say what you feel, because those who mind don’t matter and those who matter don’t mind.”
