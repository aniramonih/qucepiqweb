Siena Schoenrock, a Provo High School Student, Killed in An Auto-Pedestrian Accident

Utah: Orem community mourns Siena Schoenrock, a 17-year-old Provo High School student.

Siena Schoenrock, a 17-year-old Provo High School student, died in an auto-pedestrian accident in Orem, Utah, on Monday, October 16, 2023. The Orem community mourns its cherished member who died in a tragic accident well before her time.

What happened to Schoenrock?

According to the reports, Sienna was walking down the road when she was hit by a car. The tragic accident occurred in the area of 400 S. Vineyard Road.
According to the authorities, she was a pedestrian at 400 S. Vineyard Road when she was hit by a car. The officials said the car’s driver remained at the scene and co

operated with the investigators.

No further details are available at the moment. It is unclear whether she was pronounced dead at the scene or was transferred to the hospital and succumbed to her injuries. She lost her life in a fatal accident. Investigators are probing the incident to learn more about the circumstances that led to the accident. We will inform our readers as soon as new information comes to light.

Who was Siena Schoenrock?

Siena was a promising young woman with her whole life ahead of her. She was only 17 years old. She was a Provo High School student and a Utah Celtic FC member. She was an athlete and played soccer on the Utah Celtic team. Her loved ones describe her as kind, loving, and full of life: friend, sister, and daughter. She touched many lives with her warmth and kindness. Sadly, her life was cut short by the tragic accident, leaving all those who knew her and loved her in a deep state of sorrow.

A GoFundMe page has been established

A GoFundMe page has been established to help her family with unexpected funeral and burial expenses. The goal was set at 20,000 dollars. More than 40,000 dollars have been raised to support the family and allow them to focus on healing from their inconsolable loss.

The Community mourns Siena Schoenrock

The community has come together to mourn its beloved member. The community has outpoured support and prayers for the Schoenrock family on social media during this crucial time. They have also helped the family financially so that they can grieve their beloved daughter without worrying about the financial burden of funeral and other life expenses.

Obituary and Funeral

Seina’s parents, Alin Raquel Bauchiero and Jared Schoenrock shared the news of their beloved daughter’s death on social media. They will provide the details of funeral and burial arrangements when they are ready. They are currently taking time to recover from their grievous loss.
