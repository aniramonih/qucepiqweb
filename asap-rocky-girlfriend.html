American Rapper ASAP Rocky has a new love in his life! Is she Kendall Jenner or Someone Else?

For the fans, the dating life in Hollywood can get quite confusing. One might be sparking rumors with one person but gets into a relationship with a completely different person several months down the line. Like other rappers like Lil Wayne, Project Youngin, Nicki Minaj; ASAP Rocky is right at the center of it. 

With some paparazzi reporting that he might be getting back with his ex-flame Kendall Jenner, but in August 2019, Daily mail reported him to be in a relationship with a Brazilian Model. Are you curious to know more about the rapper new love? Then read along to know more.

Is Kendall Jenner back in his Life?

ASAP Rocky spent a month in jail in Sweden over an assault. To get him out of the prison, US President Trump lobbied and finally on August 4, 2019, he returned to the US Soil. After the release, he thanked all of his fans, labels, and friends but not to Donald Trump to which the rapper was criticized.

Subsequently, after returning to the states, the rapper and Kendall Jenner were spotted together at Kanye West's Sunday Service. As per CheatSheet, the pair talked with each other and exchanging smiles back and forth. Moreover, the rapper also sends her flowers and gifts to win her back again. But the couple ended their relationship for good the last time as they never got back together.

Prior to this, the pair were in a relationship from 2016 to 2017 and many believe they seemed very serious about each other. The 30-year-old rapper even met Kendall family but in late 2017, the pair split. To many surprise ASAP Rocky moved on with new lover Jasmine Daniels and Kendall moved on with Blake Griffin. 

The Rapper Change of Lifestyle after Kendall...

When he found his new fling in Jasmine Daniels, the rapper changed his lifestyle from being in wild parties to now focusing on something else. In an interview, the rapper admitted to letting his partner choose the parties. 

"The Gangsta Party? You know, I been chilling on some 2019 stuff. I got a girlfriend. I let her invite girls to the party, if it's necessary. I'm not in an open relationship".

He furthermore talked about #metoo movement and R. Kelly scandal as a change in his behavior. Even though he insisted on being happy in a union with model Jasmine, the romance with her remained very short. 

The Cosy Display with Model Girlfriend...

Just like his romance with Keeping up with Kardashians star his fling with Model Jasmine Daniels is also short-lived. He stayed single for a short time but as per online reports, he is officially off the market.

Rakim Mayers A.K.A. ASAP Rocky spent a romantic night with his model girlfriend and friends Singer Ciara and Zoe Kravitz. After getting released from Swedish prison, he stepped out with his new girlfriend named Daiane Sodre.

Daiane is a Brazilian model with whom the rapper started his relationship in August 2019. The Paparazzi spotted him in his blazer jacket and casual shorts hugging and kissing each other in front of Soho music venue and bar.

According to Daily Mail, the Brazilian model featured in French Montana and Drake's music video. Not just that, she also walked the ramp for designers like Oscar de la Renta and Jeremy Scott. Prior to starting her affair with the rapper, she had a fling with New England Patriots Star Julian Edelman.
