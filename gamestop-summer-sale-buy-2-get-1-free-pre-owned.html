GameStop kicks off its Summer Sale next week with Buy 2, Get 1 Free on pre-owned titles

GameStop's annual summer sale kicks off next week, which means Buy 2, Get 1 Free is back.

The GameStop Game Days Summer Sale starts on July 7, and the retailer is bringing back its Buy 2, Get 1 Free special on pre-owned games and accessories.

There will be over 200 videogames on sale with prices starting at $4.97, which is a good deal if there's anything in your backlog.

Plus, you can also save up to $40 on select new titles, such as Assassin's Creed Odyssey, Kingdom Hearts 3, and Anthem - each of which will be on sale for $19.99.

Deals on consoles will also be available. You can pick up a new PlayStation 4 1TB System for as low as $25 or $125 for a PlayStation 4 Pro 1TB System when you trade-in. And there are plenty of trade-in credit options for consoles and select mobile phones.

Wireless controllers will also be on sale along with other peripherals such as headsets. Toys and collectibles as well as t-shirts will also be available at a discount.

GameStop sent over a list of some of the games on sale, and an early look at trade-in offers. You can see those below.

The retailer's Game Days Summer Sale runs July 7-20.
