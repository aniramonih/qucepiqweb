Denise Yorks biography, fact, career, awards, net worth &amp; life story

  • Source of Wealth: Real Estate, NFL Team Owner
  • Birth Place: Youngstown, Ohio, United States
  • Marital Status: Married (John York)
  • Full Name: Marie Denise DeBartolo York
  • Nationality: American
  • Date of Birth: 1950
  • Occupation: Businesswoman
  • Education: Saint Mary’s College
  • Children: 4 (Jed, Mara, Jenna, Tony)

American businesswoman and owner of San Francisco 49ers, Denise York has a net worth of $2.5 billion. She is the daughter of real estate magnate Edward DeBartolo Sr. and the late Marie Patricia Montani DeBartolo. York scored a big victory in 2010 when the city of Santa Clara voted to approve construction of a new 68,500-seat football stadium. York is the former co-chair of the family’s private holding group, The DeBartolo Corp. She took control of the 49ers franchise in 2000 after her brother, Edward Jr., was convicted on federal bribery charges. Her son Jed, 31, is the 49ers president and CEO.
