The boy told authorities during a forensic interview that he asked Kornegay to stop but she told him to "shut up and take it like a man."

The boy stated that he was staying at Kornegay’s home and took a shower, but when he got out, Kornegay allegedly told him to “put on a dress, heels, and a bra." The report of the forensic interview that details the incident has been passed on to the Houston Police Department for further investigation.
