Becca was raised in a Jewish household but identified as agnostic, meanwhile, Austin is Christian. The couple has been feuding over religion since their honeymoon, however, they have reached the breaking point in the latest episode.

Are Austin R and Becca Haley compatible with each other?

Austin R and Becca Haley seemingly are not compatible as they hold opposite personalities and beliefs. However, in the latest episode of 'MAFS' Season 17, Becca and Austin reflected on the differences.

Becca noted that things would be different if Austin was "paired with a Christian woman." However, expert Pastor Cal insisted that they are compatible.

He explained that Austin and Becca were chosen as a match because "we felt that you would be compatible." He told the couple that the "only reason" they showed "this kind of emotion" is because of the "deep passion" they feel for one another.

Becca Haley tearfully admits she wants to continue marriage

Despite the major differences between the couple, Becca Haley wanted to continue her marriage with her husband Austin R.

In the most recent episode, Pastor Cal observed that if the couple didn't have feelings for each other, this conversation wouldn’t be taking place.

Becca tearfully replied, "I'm feeling this way because I want us to work so badly."

'MAFS' expert added, "And that’s what’s going to make you work, that is the thing that you need to hold on to."
