Change Days Season 2: A Reality Show That Tests the Limits of Love

Change Days is a Korean reality show that features four real-life couples who are on the verge of breaking up. They are given a chance to date each other’s partners for two weeks and decide whether to stay with their original partners or leave with someone new. The show is hosted by comedians Jang Do Yeon and Yang Se Chan, and musicians Code Kunst and Heo Young Ji.

What Happens in Change Days Season 2?

Change Days Season 2 aired from June 2, 2022 to September 15, 2022 on Kakao TV and Netflix. The four couples who participated in the show were:

  • Choi Yun Seul and Min Hyo Gi, who have been dating for six years but have grown apart due to their busy schedules and lack of communication.
  • Lee Jung Hoon and Kim Ji Eun, who have been dating for three years but have different personalities and values.
  • Park Ji Hyun and Kim Min Jae, who have been dating for two years but have frequent conflicts and trust issues.
  • Lee Soo Jin and Kim Tae Hyun, who have been dating for a year but have lost their passion and excitement.

The show followed the couples as they swapped partners and went on various dates, such as camping, cooking, stargazing, and playing games. The dates were designed to create a romantic atmosphere and test the compatibility of the new couples. The original couples also had to face each other and confront their problems, as well as watch their partners interact with their dates.
